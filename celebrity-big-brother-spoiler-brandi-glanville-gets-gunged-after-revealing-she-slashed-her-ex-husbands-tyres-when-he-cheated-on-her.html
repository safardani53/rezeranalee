Celebrity Big Brother spoiler: Brandi Glanville gets gunged after revealing she slashed her ex husba

Publish date: 2024-02-01

CELEBRITY Big Brother's Brandi Glanville was gunged in a messy new challenge after it emerged she slashed her cheating ex-husband's tyres.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star's revenge plan was outed during the latest task, which split the house into two teams and asked them to match random facts to the housemate they were about.

The idea was that if the housemates on team one guessed the correct person the fact was about in team two, then that person would be gunged by 'champagne'.

Unfortunately for the celebrities, it wasn't the sort of bubbles they are used to, instead the 'champagne' was actually a thick yellow gungy liquid.

One of the facts about a housemate, read out by Love Island legend Paul Danan, was: "Slashed ex-husband's tyres when he cheated on her."

Immediately the housemates voted for Brandi, 44, who has been vocal already about actor ex Eddie Cibrian cheating on her with his now-wife LeAnn Rimes.

And they were correct, as a stream of liquid immediately spewed out over Brandi's head.

Understandably, she didn't look entirely impressed.

Brandi, who has two children with her ex-husband, has spoken out in the past about slashing Eddie's tyres after he had an affair with country singer LeAnn.

In an interview with Glamour in 2013, she admitted: "I got a knife, and I went out to the garage. I just took it to both of his Harleys.

"I hated those bikes—my dad had almost died in a motorcycle accident.

"I had never wanted Eddie on them, because we had children.

"I slashed all of the tyres, and it felt so good."

Brandi also spoke about the incident in an episode of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and said she immediately called Eddie afterwards to advise him of what she'd done and not to ride the motorcycles.

Brandi referenced Eddie and LeAnn's affair on her arrival to the CBB house on Tuesday.

She took a dig at LeAnn by taking a pause in the middle of the word 'country' which turned it into the word 'c***'.

Talking about Eddie, she said: "He married a c***ry music star after they cheated together, and now they are happy.''

Brandi and Eddie were married from 2001 until 2010.

They split when it emerged he'd been having an affair with LeAnn on the set of their 2009 TV movie Northern Lights.

Singer LeAnn had also been married at the time of the affair, to chef Dean Sheremet, and told People magazine in 2010: "I did one of the most selfish things that I possibly could do, in hurting someone else.

"I take responsibility for everything I’ve done. I hate that people got hurt.

“But I don’t regret the outcome.”

Eddie and LeAnn then wed in 2011.
