Wheel of Fortune One Letter Puzzle Solve by Caitlin Burke (PHOTOS, VIDEO)

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Wheel of Fortune contestant Caitlin Burke solved a 27-letter, 7-word prize puzzle with one letter and an apostrophe showing. To say that show host Pat Sajak was flabbergasted is an understatement.

The normal strategy for contestants is to rack up as much money as possible before solving – even when they know the answer. Because it was a puzzle that would reap Burke a prize, she opted to solve the puzzle after only one letter was revealed.

The NYC fashion editor’s guess was: I’ve got a good feeling about this. When Sajak asked her how she figured it out, she responded: “I had a good feeling about it.”

Her prize turned out to be a Caribbean vacation to Granada, worth $6,468.
