I'm a bargain hunter and I love Farmfoods - I always find the best hidden treasures and it's so chea

Publish date: 2024-08-28

A BARGAIN hunter has taken to social media to show off her epic Farmfoods shopping haul.

The savvy shopper, Jess (@jessdoinglifee), regularly shares shopping videos to TikTok and her latest clip has left many very impressed.

If you are looking to cut back on your food shop where possible, you’ll want to get down to a Farmfoods quick. 

Farmfoods is a British frozen food and grocery supermarket chain based in Cumbernauld, Scotland. 

There are over 300 shops in the United Kingdom, of which more than a hundred are in Scotland.

Jess was left very excited when she did a food shop in her local Farmfoods.

She said: “Is it just me who gets excited about going to supermarkets late at night?

“Especially Farmfoods, because I think Farmfoods is one of those shops, it's like hidden treasure.

“You never know what you're gonna get, or there might be things that you've not seen before or things that you've not seen in a long time.”

As she proceeded to walk around the shop, picking up bargain buys along the way, she added: “Choc Dips are my absolute favourite things in the world. They are absolutely unreal.

“I mean, look at these wafer rolls, Oreos, they're going in the basket.

“These mix-ups are unreal as well and if you haven't had this garlic butter yet, get to know, it is the one.

“Now, I've not seen this sauce [Havana Club] before, but when I saw it, I was like, yes, that would be absolutely unreal with some of the meals that I cook.

“And then I just got these [Chicago Town pizzas]. Banging.” 

Jess’ clip has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 102k views.

Many social media users revealed that they too love shopping at Farmfoods, whilst others explained that they were eager to visit.
