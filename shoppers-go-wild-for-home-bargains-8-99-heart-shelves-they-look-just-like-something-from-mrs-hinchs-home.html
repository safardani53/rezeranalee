Shoppers go wild for Home Bargains 8.99 heart shelves &amp; they look just like something from Mrs

Publish date: 2024-04-07

GREY homes have been on-trend for quite some time, with fans of cleaning influencer Mrs Hinch looking to emulate the queen of clean's immaculate home.

So it's no surprise that a set of heart-shaped shelves - that look JUST like something Mrs Hinch would have in her grey home - have proved VERY popular with shoppers.

The stylish storage comes in white or grey, while both colours feature a mirrored background.

Home decor fans have gone wild for the shelves, which are priced at £8.99 from Home Bargains, sharing snaps of their own sets at home.

Some have adorned their heart shelves with plants, while others chose candles, vases or other trinkets to decorate.

The Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK has been awash with images of the heart-shaped shelves at home.

Not content with just one hue, many happy customers pledged to go out and get the other option.

One woman wrote: "I've got the grey one. I need the white next!"

Another chimed in: "Got the white one today, already got the grey."

Meanwhile, others complained of not being able to find the shelves anywhere because they are so frequently snapped up by other shoppers.

"'I've looked for months," complained one woman, while another replied, "they are like gold dust aren’t they?!"

What's more, some people claimed that the shelves are such hot property that they're being re-sold online for up to three times the price.

"Someone is selling these on my local selling site for £20," one woman said.

Another added: "They are £25 on eBay lol."

If you want to get your hands on the Home Bargains shelves, you'll have to be quick - they're selling like hot cakes.
