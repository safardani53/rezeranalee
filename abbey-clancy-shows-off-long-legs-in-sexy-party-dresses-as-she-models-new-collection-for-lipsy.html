Abbey Clancy shows off long legs in sexy party dresses from her new Lipsy range

Publish date: 2024-09-12

ABBEY Clancy looked sensational as she modelled her new Lipsy range just in time for the party season.

The gorgeous model flashed her trademark long legs as she showed off her latest collection with the clothing brand.

Abbey, 32, was a sight for sore eyes as she posed in everything from sexy mini dresses to glamorous gowns with racy splits.

One particularly saucy snap showed the wife of Peter Crouch standing in a sparkly thigh-skimming frock with her legs spread apart.

Another glamorous image showed Abbey showing off her lean limbs in as she sits on the corner of a sofa in a super stylish fur coat.

Proving even chunky jumpers can be sexy, the beauty teamed her knits with leather look leggings and sequin-encrusted pencil skirts.

Lipsy announced they were teaming up with the TV presenter last month for the AW18 Abbey Clancy X Lipsy collection.

Her debut will launch with more than 20 pieces, with emphasis on figure-hugging silhouettes, tailored fabrics and lots of sparkles.

The collection will be available to purchase from today.

Aside from a fashion launch, Abbey will also appear on Gogglebox's charity special alongside husband Peter and dad Geoff  on Friday night.
