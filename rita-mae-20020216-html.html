"Rita May" is a song by Bob Dylan, originally recorded during the Desire album showcase, but released only on side B of a single and in the masterpieces composite album. The song is based on the 1957 rockabilly song "Bertha Lou". "Rita May" was covered by Jerry Lee Lewis in his 1979 album Jerry Lee Lewis.
