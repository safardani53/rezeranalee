Kylie Jenner goes completely nude and covers herself in blood in new photos for Nightmare on Elm Str

Publish date: 2024-03-26

KYLIE Jenner stripped off and drenched herself in fake blood for her latest makeup collection.

The horror-inspired Elm Street collection, launches on Tuesday, just in time for Halloween.

Kylie, 24, shared two gory snaps to her Instagram account, showing her sat on the floor naked and dripping with blood.

The star could be seen sat on plastic sheeting and gazing at the camera with the fake stuff poured all down her front and legs.

The pregnant star captioned the photos: "MY KYLIE X NIGHTMARE ON ELM STREET COLLECTION LAUNCHES TOMORROW! 3pm pst KylieCosmetics.com"

The KUWTK star received mixed feedback about her "spooky" new cosmetics line inspired by the horror film Nightmare on Elm Street. 

When she first announced the collection last week, Kylie wrote: "ahhh what a DREAM to collaborate with the icon of horror!"

Freddy Krueger is the gruesome protagonist of the 1984 cult horror A Nightmare on Elm Street, who stalks and kills people in their dreams.

The makeup mogul also shared some inside peaks of the collection on her Instagram Stories, showing off the Halloween-themed packaging.

The beauty kit launches on October 12, featuring various shades of lipsticks, eyelashes, and a "bloody" jelly stain.

FANS NOT CONVINCED

While many were excited for the spooky collaboration to drop, others weren't convinced that the Freddie Kruger look was what they wanted to emulate.

One user commented: "I keep laughing at 'kylie cosmetics' 'collaborating with nightmare on elm street'"

A second echoed the statement writing: "nightmare on elm street x kylie .... nope nope nope nope nope nope"

A third simply replied: "This is a no"

While another critic wasn't sold on the idea either commenting: "Kylie x nightmare on elm street ..... are you f***ing kidding me ....."

Gossip account, Not Skinny Not Fat, also chimed in with their thoughts by sharing the promo pic with the caption: "Absolutely no one:"

They also commented on the lash set that Kylie gave a glimpse of in her Stories, seemingly not impressed.

They wrote: "Freddie Kruger lashes!?? Why??? Ok sorry I know I'm sh***ing on Halloween and a lot of you hate me for it but it's just. Not for me lol"

PREGNANCY REVEAL

Back in September, the young mom revealed that she's pregnant with her second child following weeks of fan speculation.

She is already mother to three-year-old daughter Stormi with boyfriend Travis Scott, 30.

As part of the pregnancy announcement, Kylie shared a video that included footage from a visit to the doctor for an ultrasound with Travis and their daughter.

Later in the clip, the couple surprised momager and family matriarch Kris Jenner with the exciting news by handing over a photo of the ultrasound.
