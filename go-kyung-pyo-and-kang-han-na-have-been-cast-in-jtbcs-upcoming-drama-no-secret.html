Go Kyung-pyo And Kang Han-na Have Been Cast In JTBCs Upcoming Drama No Secret

Publish date: 2024-06-08

No Secrets is a new JTBC romantic drama starring Go Kyung-pyo and Kang Ha-na (working title). On February 14, it was announced that the two would play the protagonists in the forthcoming comedic romance about a love tale between a variety show writer and an announcer. No Secrets will be produced by KEYEAST, a division of SM Entertainment. Meanwhile, Jang Ji-yeon will direct the K-drama, with Choi Kyung-sun writing the script. No Secret, a cheeky romance with a dark comic undertone, chronicles the lives of announcer Song Ki-baek, who has a strange sickness that causes him to talk without thinking, leading him to get involved in the affairs of variety show writer On Woo-ju.

No Secrets, a new romantic drama from JTBC, will feature Go Kyung-pyo and Kang Ha-na.

The broadcasting station revealed the character descriptions for both Go Kyung-pyo and Kang Han-na, along with other details. Go Kyung-pyo will play Song Ki-baek, an announcer renowned for adhering to the laws and maintaining a clean image in society. Things change one day when he has an unintentional condition that leads him to talk without thinking. After meeting On Woo-ju, his second innings in life became more exciting.

The actor was most recently seen in Love In Contract, co-starring with Park Min-young. He has already shown his versatility as an actor in a number of famous dramas such as Reply 1988, Chicago Typewriter, Connect, and others. Kang Han-na will portray On Woo-ju, a motivated and ardent variety show writer who would go to any length to ensure the success of the entertainment program. She chooses to go on a dating variety show with Song Ki-baek after learning his peculiar style of speaking. Fans have previously praised the actress for her roles in Start-Up, My Roommate Is a Gumiho, Bloody Heart, and others. The rest of the cast has yet to be disclosed by the production company.

Fans are anxiously anticipating the pair’s performances in the next love drama. Furthermore, they have already turned to Twitter to express their admiration for Go Kyung-pyo and Kang Han-na.

No Secrets will premiere in 2023, while no specific date has been set. Recently, KEYEAST Entertainment revealed:

“The particular broadcast days and hours for 2023 are still being worked upon with JTBC. [. . .] Keyeast is demonstrating its capacity to create K-content by distributing it to a variety of platforms, including terrestrial, comprehensive, and worldwide OTT (internet video service).”

No Secret, Trigger, Ask The Stars, Illumination, Ring Marvell, From The Gaze, and 1/N is a Secret are among the films slated for KEYEAST audiences.
