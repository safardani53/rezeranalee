Who Is Nikita Kuzmin? Meet Layton William Dance Partner on Strictly Come Dancing 2023

Publish date: 2024-06-04

Layton Williams, a West End legend, has paired up with Nikita Kuzmin to become the first all-male dancing couple on Strictly Come Dancing 2023. As of October 1st, 2023, their dynamic relationship has not only captivated spectators but also marked a watershed event in the show’s history.

Given the obvious enthusiasm around their collaboration, learning more about their histories gives insights into their journey and the joy that they bring to the dance floor. Layton Williams, best known for his appearances in Billy Elliot the Musical and Bad Education, adds his flair to the stage. Nikita Kuzmin, who has been a regular on the Strictly Come Dancing stage since 2021, will be his partner.

Layton Williams’ Impressive CV

Williams is not your average contender. His many contributions to the entertainment industry speak for itself. He’s a force to be reckoned with, having been in renowned West End productions such as Wicked and Dreamland. Furthermore, Layton’s television appearances have increased his visibility.

Nikita Kuzmin’s Point of View

This collaboration with Williams presents an exciting challenge for Nikita Kuzmin. He has indicated a strong desire to guide Williams through the complexities of ballroom and Latin dances. Given Williams’ dancing talents, Kuzmin feels their pairing can stun and prevail.

Layton William’s Previous Dance Experience

Layton William is no stranger to the dance floor. His ballet and modern training, along with performances in shows such as The Nutcracker, is excellent. Ballroom and Latin dances, on the other hand, are new territory for him, providing a fascinating depth to his adventure on Strictly Come Dancing.

Reactions of the Audience

The pair has received a lot of love. Their on-stage chemistry, representation of the LGBTQ+ community, and unrivaled skill have propelled them to the top of the Glitterball Trophy race.

Same-Sex Pairing

Layton William and Nikita Kuzmin’s collaboration goes beyond simply performance. It is a tribute to LGBTQ+ representation on prime-time television. Layton’s openness about his sexuality, as well as his delight in representing the LGBTQ+ community in the competition, lends dimension to their dancing performances.

Conclusion

Layton Williams and Nikita Kuzmin’s collaboration is more than simply a dancing team; it’s a celebration of skill, representation, and breaking down boundaries. Their progress on Strictly Come Dancing 2023 is being closely followed, with many cheering for them to succeed.
