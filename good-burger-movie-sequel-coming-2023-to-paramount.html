Good Burger 2 Streaming November 22 On Paramount+

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Paramount+ is serving up Good Burger 2 with Kenan Thompson and Kel Mitchell, streaming exclusively on Paramount+ November 22.

Welcome back to Good Burger, home of the Good Burger!

Paramount+ and Nickelodeon Studios have announced Good Burger 2, an all-new original movie sequel to the iconic '90s feature film based on the sketch from the comedy series All That. The sequel, coming exclusively to Paramount+ on November 22 follows Dexter Reed's (Kenan Thompson) and Ed's (Kel Mitchell) reunion at the popular fast-food restaurant, Good Burger.

STREAM: The Original Good Burger Now on Paramount+

In Good Burger 2, Dexter Reed is down on his luck after another one of his inventions fails. Ed welcomes Dex back to "Good Burger" with open arms and gives him his old job back. With a new crew working at Good Burger, Dex devises a plan to get back on his feet but unfortunately puts the fate of Good Burger at risk once again. 

Production of Good Burger 2 began in May 2023, with a Paramount+ exclusive premiere November 22.

The original '90s sketch first aired on All That in 1994 and was centered around the fast-food restaurant, Good Burger and its clueless cashier, Ed, played by Kel Mitchell in Seasons 1-5. The feature film Good Burger was released worldwide in 1997. In the All That revival series in 2019, Mitchell returned guest starring as Ed, who has been working at Good Burger for the past 20 years as manager of the trainees.

"Ed is one of those timeless characters that has brought so much joy and laughter to fans, and it's such a beautiful blessing to still be doing it after all these years," said Mitchell. "It's super awesome to come back and continue the stories of Dex, Ed, and Good Burger for both the people who grew up watching and their kids."

Get ready for the return of Good Burger on November 22 by brushing up on your All That knowledge, along with its popular spinoffs, Kenan & Kel, The Amanda Show, and the revival series, All That (2019). It's waiting for you now on Paramount+.
