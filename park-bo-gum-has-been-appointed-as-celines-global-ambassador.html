Park Bo-gum Has Been Appointed As Celines Global Ambassador

Publish date: 2024-05-23

W Korea announced on Wednesday, November 23, 2022, that South Korean actress Park Bo-gum has joined the French luxury brand Celine as their worldwide ambassador. W Korea said on Instagram that the 29-year-old is the first male actor to join the Parisian brand’s family. The tweet also explained how he debuted his new profession as an E-boy in Celine’s new 2022 Boy Doll collection.

Since his release from the military earlier this year, the actor has been involved in a variety of elements of the Korean entertainment business, including TVING’s reality program Youth MT. With the announcement of his formal return to the fashion business, fans are anticipating a slew of fresh material after his two-year hiatus.

Celine has appointed Park Bo-gum as their first male worldwide spokesperson.

Park Bo-gum has been chosen Celine’s global brand ambassador, joining BLACKPINK’s Lisa. This announcement follows the Hello Monster star’s June debut at Celine’s Man’s Summer 2023 at Men’s Paris Fashion Week. While Celine invited him and BTS’ V to the concert, he will be attending their future events as their worldwide ambassador. With his new title on the way, the actor showed off his charm while rocking the Boy Doll line. With his article in W Korea’s December issue, he expertly achieved the Rock & Roll and Glam Punk aesthetics. Fans are thrilled with the announcement and want to see more of him as he proudly continues his work as Celine’s new worldwide ambassador, despite his lengthy absence from K-dramas. After seeing flashes of his clothes during Paris Fashion Week in June, fans are eager to get their hands on more Park Bo-gum stuff.

What can we expect from Celine’s upcoming collection?

Celine debuted their new Indie Summer vacation 2023 collection in Saint Tropez. The collection, designed by Hedi Slimane, revives numerous forgotten fashion trends, making them appear as fresh as ever. The runway has a lot of horizontal stripes on cropped sweaters, which are traditional for resort collections, but this time coupled with tiny shorts. To add to the intrigue, slim jeans, originally created by Hedi Slimane, are making a reappearance. The Japanese denim spray-on jeans are worn with loose shirts and scarves, creating a nostalgic and interesting aesthetic for the collection.

In other developments, actor Park Bo-gum has been involved in fields other than fashion. The actor appeared in TVING’s reality program Youth MT: Young Actors’ Retreat, among numerous well-known K-drama actors such as Park Seo-joon, Hwang In-yeop, Ji Chang-wook, and Kim Yoo-jung. He also took part in Raise Korea’s temperature campaign, transporting 12 briquettes to impoverished communities. Fans are both proud and curious about Park Bo-new gum’s adventure as a worldwide ambassador for Celine, and they anxiously await it.
