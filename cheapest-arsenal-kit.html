The cheapest place to buy Arsenals 2019/20 kit

Publish date: 2024-03-16

ARSENAL switched shirt sponsors for the 2019/20 season, replacing Puma with Adidas.

And if you want to snap up the fresh new designs from the German kit-maker, here's how you can do it the cheapest.

The Gunners have simplified the design for their home kit this season, replacing the multi-toned red design of last year with a block colours.

But while the home kit is not too exciting, both the away kit and the third kit are making waves.

Away from the Emirates this season, Arsenal will be wearing a re-make of the famous 'bruised banana kit' that they wore between 1991 and 1993.

And the third kit is an attractive blue and gold number (that's pretty similar to Real Madrid's away kit... especially since they have the same make and sponsor).

All three kits will set you back £60 when bought through Arsenal Direct.

But if you shop around you can find them cheaper elsewhere.

Sports Direct stock most Premier League kits £5 cheaper than the official club shops.

And Amazon has a number of the new season's kit in stock for varying prices which can beat what you'll get at Sports Direct.

If you want to get the most for your money, though, it's also worth while making your purchase through a cashback site such as Quidco or TopCashback.
