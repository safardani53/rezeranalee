Primarks Chip mug is back in stores.... with this one adorable new feature

Publish date: 2024-08-26

LAST year, Primark dropped a product that caused such a stir amongst Disney fans, it sold out almost everywhere.

The cute Chip mug from their Beauty And The Beast collection was so endearing that eager shoppers were desperate to get their hands on one – and now, they’re back and even cuter than before.

Chip is the ceramic son of Mrs Potts from the Disney film, and is a popular character due to his cheeky lines and rosy grin.

The original mug featured the tiny chip on the rim of the mug for that extra realistic touch, and Primark’s new option looks like he’s extra pleased.

Instead of a bright-eyed cheeky grin, the cartoon character has flushed cheeks and is squinting his eyes – perhaps somebody has told him that he makes a great cup of tea.

The even more adorable mug costs just £5, so is the same price as the popular one last year – and will make a great friend to the previous one, if you were lucky enough to get hold of it.

If last year is anything to go by, you may want to start sprinting to the shops now.

It’s available in UK stores, so hit the high-street this weekend for the cute cup – or surprise a Disney-obsessed pal with it.

The only thing that’s better than a perfect cup of tea is enjoying it in the perfect cup, and this is perfect to slurp from during a Disney marathon on the weekend.
