Molly Young is a Global Entertainment and Lifestyle Writer at GRV Media with a specialty in influencer news, TikTok, Snapchat and Instagram. She works across several publications and is constantly evolving her journalism skills through a degree in Journalism with Public Relations at Leeds Beckett University. Boasting a creative background through her studies and having completed a one-year internship with Disney, Molly loves creating content and providing entertainment analysis through her writing. She is the chief influencer reporter on Celebrity Tidbit and could win any Addison Rae trivia quiz. If the celeb is famous because of an @ before their name then Molly is the expert.
