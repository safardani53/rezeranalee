Emily Maynard Wedding Video Shows Ceremony With Tyler Johnson

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Emily Maynard is a bachelorette no more! The gorgeous reality star, 28, tied the knot last weekend, marrying her love Tyler Johnson in a surprise ceremony on Saturday, June 7, at her new husband’s parents’ estate in South Carolina. 

The former Bachelorette beauty gave an inside peek at the nuptials via her Instagram account on Tuesday, June 17, sharing a short video from the wedding. She captioned the clip, “…oh heavenly day…” 

In the clip—created by Heart Stone Films, which also shared the video online—Maynard is seen in her pink-hued custom Modern Trousseau strapless wedding gown, which featured rosette embellishments and a belt at the waist. 

“Dearly beloved, we are gathered here this evening to marry Tyler Johnson and Emily Maynard,” the officiant says in the clip, as the camera pans to Maynard’s 8-year-old daughter Ricki, shots of Maynard walking down the aisle, the romantic table settings complete with peonies and twinkling tea candles, and the pink-and-gold wedding cake.

Johnson and Maynard—who took Johnson’s last name when she said “I do”—are then shown at the altar, before they dance as husband and wife. 

“It was romantic and beautiful,” an insider told Us Weekly of the ceremony. “When Tyler saw Emily, he was completely in awe.”
