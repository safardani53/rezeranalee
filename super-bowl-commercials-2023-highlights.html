Highlights From The Celebrity Ad Spots Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-29

With the teams set for Super Bowl LVII (Philadelphia Eagles and Kansas City Chiefs), it’s time to focus on the real reason to watch the Big Game, besides the Rihanna Halftime Show: the commercials! With most of the ad space sold out by September 2022, the Feb. 12 game promises to be bigger, bolder, and more star-studded than ever. So, it’s only right that the commercials go over the top this year.

Take, for instance, the ‘Caddyshack’ inspired commercial from Michelob Ultra. Brian Cox and Serena William, pictured here, are two new members at Bushwood Country Club. Alex Morgan, Canelo Alvarez, and Tony Romo (doing his best Carl Spackler impression) are also part of the ad, continuing the brand’s tradition of marrying cult classic movies with sports stars.

Michelob Ultra isn’t the only star-packed ad this year – far from it. From Meghan Trainor getting stuck in a Pringles can to Maya Rudolph trying to get people to forget about sexy M&Ms, to Anna Faris being a naked Eve in a garden of Avocados of Mexico, it’s all wild. Check out the highlights.
