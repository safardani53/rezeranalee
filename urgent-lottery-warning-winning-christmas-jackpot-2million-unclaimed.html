Urgent lottery warning as winning Christmas jackpot worth $2million unclaimed after being bought in

Publish date: 2024-03-05

A LOTTERY ticket worth $2million remains unclaimed over Christmas weekend. 

The Kentucky Lottery is calling for the winner to claim the prize for the Wednesday, December 20 Powerball drawing. 

The winning numbers are 27, 35, 41, 56, 60.

The Powerball is 16.

The ticket was sold at the Five Star Convenience Store in Radcliff, Kentucky. 

The Kentucky Lottery will be closed on Monday for Christmas Day but will reopen on Tuesday, the Lexington Herald Lead reported.
