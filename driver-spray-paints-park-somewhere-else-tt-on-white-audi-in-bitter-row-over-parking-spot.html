Driver spray-paints 'park elsewhere t**t' on white Audi in bitter row over parking spot

Publish date: 2024-07-28

A WHITE Audi has been plastered with a rude message in an apparent parking row turned nasty.

The luxury vehicle was spray-painted with the message "park elsewhere t**t" across its bonnet, with the smutty note leaving witnesses stunned.

Meg House spotted the white Audi covered in the crass message as she walked home in Hampshire last week, saying she believed it had been inspired by an out-of-control parking dispute.

The 22-year-old said: "I was just absolutely stunned – it's criminal damage.

"It's just completely horrible. Such a horrible thing to do to a person's car."

She said that while she suspected the owner of the Audi had been a repeat offender in the parking dispute, it was an "over the top" reaction.

She said: "That car perhaps shouldn't have been parked there but to write that over the car is a little bit over the top.

"I just can't believe someone would do that."

While the car was spotted outside the Winchester Constabulary's Operational headquarters, police have confirmed they were not investigating or involved in the matter.

But Meg, a senior administrative assistant, said: "I would be very annoyed if that was my car. If it was mine I would have certainly reported it to the police.

"I suppose it looked a bit like spray paint but the ink was running in the rain – I would like to think the rain has washed it off."

Meg said the car had since disappeared.

A spokesperson for Hampshire Constabulary said: "We are not investigating a report of criminal damage connected to this car.

"This is not a police vehicle."
