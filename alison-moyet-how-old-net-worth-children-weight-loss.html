ALISON Moyet is back with her ninth studio album and her biggest world tour in three decades.

But in which synthpop duo did she start her career in and what has she said about weight loss? Here's all we know...

Who is Alison Moyet and what is she best known for?

Alison Moyet - born Geneviève Alison Jane Moyet - is a 56-year-old English singer and songwriter best known for her work in synthpop duo Yazoo and as a solo artist.

An Essex native, Alison was involved in a number of local punk rock bands during her teenage years while juggling jobs as a shop assistant and piano tuner.

Alison is famed for her deep, soulful voice and self-penned lyrics.

Who were Yazoo and what were their biggest hits?

Yazoo were a British synthpop duo consisting of Alison Moyet and Vince Clarke.

They released two albums, Upstairs at Eric's (1982) and You and Me Both (1983), which gave Yazoo a number of Top 3 hits including Only You, Don't Go, Situation and The Other Side Of Love.

Yazoo split in 1983, citing a personality clash and creative differences for the break-up.

Alison and Vince reunited in 2008 for a string of live dates and to celebrate the reissue of their discography.

What are Alison Moyet's biggest solo hits?

Alison Moyet began her solo career immediately after Yazoo's split in 1983.

She’s since sold over 23 million albums and scored nine Top 30 singles.

Her most streamed songs on Spotify include All Cried Out, Love Resurrection, Is This Love? and Love Letters.

Alison’s latest album Other was released in June 2017 and she will embark on a world tour later in the year.

What has Alison Moyet said about her weight loss battle?

Alison has spoken openly about her battle with weight loss and previously admitted to losing too much weight following an intense diet that saw her go from a size 22 to a size ten.

Speaking to The Telegraph in 2013, Alison revealed: "Personally I think women look better with a bit of chub on them. I've lost too much weight and I'm working on getting fatter again.

"It was a mixture of health reasons and not wanting to be looked after by the patronising thin.

"It's funny as my whole character has been based upon not getting approval elsewhere. And I don't like the kind of approval that goes with weight loss."

Does Alison Moyet have children and is she married?

Alison is married to David Ballard with whom she has done daughter, Caitlin, 21.

She is also mother to son Joe, 32, and daughter Alex, 30, from previous relationships.

She currently resides in East Sussex.

What is Alison Moyet's net worth and how did she make her money?

Alison Moyet's net worth is in the region of £8.2 million as reported by Richest Celebrities.

She will have earned royalties through her music as both the main songwriter and performer.

Alison's also enjoyed strong sales of greatest hits sets and reissues of classic albums.
