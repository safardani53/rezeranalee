Who is Zo Sugg? YouTube star, Joe Sugg's sister who's dating Alfie Deyes

Publish date: 2024-03-12

ZOELLA was one of the first influencers to make a successful career in social media.

Her success even saw her named the most powerful beauty vlogger by Forbes Magazine in 2017.

Who is Zoë Sugg?

Zoella, real name Zoë Sugg, was born on March 28, 1990, and is currently 32 years old.

Her younger brother, Joe Sugg, is also a vlogger and star of Strictly.

She gained A-levels in art, photography, and textiles, but did not attend university due to her anxiety.

She began her career as a YouTuber in 2009 filming videos while doing an internship in interior design.

She would document her daily life and write about makeup and fashion, as well as collaborate with other influencers such as Louise Pentland and Marcus Butler.

Many of her fans loved her original content which included shopping, Lush hauls, and makeup tutorials.

As of September 2023, she has over 10.7 million subscribers.

What is Zoë Sugg's net worth?

The English fashion and beauty vlogger reportedly has a net worth of around $4million.

She made a majority of her wealth from her fashion and beauty blog and her YouTube channel, but her published books and make-up line have also contributed.

Her debut novel Girl Online was released in November 2014.

A sequel, Girl Online: On Tour was released in 2015, and a third entitled Girl Online: Going Solo was released in November 2016.

In 2014, she launched Zoella Beauty, a brand of beauty products that was described as the "biggest beauty launch of the year" and not long after, she unveiled a range of lifestyle products.

What is Zoë Sugg's Instagram?

Zoë’s Instagram account currently boasts more than 9.2 million followers.

There she shares "behind the scenes, beauty, lifestyle, community & positivity."

She's also on Twitter with the username @zoella where she has almost 200k followers.

On YouTube, she can also be found posting under Zoella.

Who is Zoë Sugg dating?

Zoë is dating the equally famous YouTuber Alfie Deyes who started his YouTube channel at the age of just 15.

He was signed to the talent agency Gleam Futures up until 2017.

The pair have been together since 2013.

On September 14, 2023, the couple announced they were engaged via a post on Instagram. The post was a black-and-white video of them kissing and looking at mountains.

Does Zoë Sugg have any children?

Zoë and Alfie announced in March 2021 that they are expecting their first child together.

The 32-year-old shared the “super exciting news” with her millions of followers on Instagram on March 6.

Sugg shared a video of herself flashing her growing bump followed by footage of her first sonogram.
