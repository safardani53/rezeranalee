Haley Van Voorhis Boyfriend: Is First Female NCAA Kicker Dating?

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Fans of NCAA football have been curious about Haley Van Voorhis’ boyfriend. Following her historic achievement in NCAA football, Voorhis has been all over the internet. 

With the footballer’s sudden rise to fame, there is not much information about her personal life. Meanwhile, there have been no official records of her ever dating anyone. 

Furthermore, her social media posts also do not indicate that the athlete is dating anyone in particular at the moment. 

Haley Van Voorhis, born and brought up in Virginia, is a National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) football player who plays for the Shenandoah University Hornets.

On September 23, 2023, the Hornets safety became the first woman to play in a position other than kicker in NCAA football at any level. 

The 20-year-old played football, basketball, and lacrosse in her Christchurch School in Virginia. As the wide receiver and defensive back, Voorhis was 2019 All-State Honorable Mention in Virginia. 

Further, Voorhis played for the D.C. Divas as the strong safety in the Pro division of the Women’s Football Alliance in Washington D.C., where she recorded 21 tackles in 5 games. 

On September 23, 2023, Voorhis made a new record when she played in the Hornets’ game against Juniata Eagles, becoming the first woman to play in a position other than kicker. 

Moreover, at just 5-foot-6 and 145 pounds, she was able to register a quarterback hurry.

Haley Van Voorhis Boyfriend: Who Is The Hornets Star Dating?

Haley Van Voorhis doesn’t seem to have a boyfriend at the moment.

Besides having no official announcement of her dating anyone, the Hornets star hasn’t posted any pictures on her social media that might suggest that she is dating someone. 

However, that is not to say that she is not dating anyone. She might be in a discreet relationship or it might just suggest her desire for maintaining a private life.

Moreover, most of her interviews indicate that she is focused on her career, trying to get better and keep smashing records.  

So, it might be safe to assume that she is not looking to date anyone in particular currently. 

It is no surprise that the fans want to know who the new star is dating. However, with no information about her personal life available, perhaps it is best to just celebrate the rising star’s record-breaking achievement.

Road To Creating History

Heidi, Haley’s mother, remembers all too well when her daughter, as a toddler, asked her why there weren’t any girls on the field.

That question made her speechless, however, it paved the way for Haley to become an athlete. 

Voorhis was never the one to be discouraged by the lack of girls playing football. Instead, it came to her as a surprise and made her want to be the one playing football.

Her father, Chandler, remembers going to watch football games with the family when his daughter would tell the family that she would be in the field someday. 

Furthermore, Voorhis’ parents claim that their daughter has always been determined and hard-working.

She was the first girl to play football at her high school as well. When the boys in her team started getting big, Voorhis would sweat out in the gym to get even stronger. 

In a recent interview, she suggested younger girls who look up to her to expect the journey to be extremely hard, physically and mentally. 

The 20-year-old has moved on from this record-breaking feat and is now focused on her next challenge. She is focused on getting opportunities to play more and be a better football player.
