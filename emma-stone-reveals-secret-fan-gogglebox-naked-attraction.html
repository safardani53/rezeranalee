Huge Hollywood A-list star reveals she's a secret fan of Gogglebox and Naked Attraction

Publish date: 2024-07-21

HOLLYWOOD star Emma Stone has revealed her favourite British shows - and they might not be what you expect.

The La La Land actress made the surprising admission as she toured the world for her latest project.

Emma, 35, is one of the most acclaimed actresses of her generation with an Academy Award, a BAFTA, A Golden Globe and and three Screen Actors Guild Awards under her belt.

With dozens more nominations and constant projects on the go, the actress is busier than ever.

But as it turns out, if she is not on set filming another blockbuster movie, she likes to binge on British TV in her down time.

Speaking on the New Year's special of The Graham Norton show, she admitted: "My foray into it began with The Only Way is Essex of course, and then I learned all about Naked Attraction and Gogglebox.

"I became completely crazy about it and in America the two shows are a double feature. They are back-to-back so it’s cosy family viewing followed by nakedness. I love that!"

As usual, Virgin Radio presenter Graham, 60, took a look back at some of the most memorable stories, events and guests on his eponymous chat show in 2023.

He was joined by a star studded line-up which included The Favourite actress, her new co-star Mark Ruffalo, Strictly Come Dancing host Claudia Winkleman, Gavin & Stacey star Rob Brydon, and the Mercury Prize-winning Ezra Collective.

Although some thought she appeared tired on the promotional tour, Emma couldn't help but gush about her newest project Poor Things.
