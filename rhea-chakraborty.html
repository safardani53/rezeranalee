Rhea Chakraborty’s Age & Early Life

Rhea Chakraborty was born on July 1, 1992, in Bangalore, Karnataka. She is of Asian descent with Bengali ancestors. She is 31 years old as of 2023. Her zodiac sign is also Cancer. Career

She got her start in the entertainment sector by appearing on MTV India’s TVS Scooty Teen Diva. She finished as the first runner-up in the program. She then worked as a VJ in the entertainment industry. She has hosted many MTV series as a VJ, including TicTac College Beat, MTV Gone in 60 Seconds, and Pepsi MTV Wassup. She then began appearing in several TV ads, including Yepme New and Stayfree.

She made her Telugu cinema debut in 2012 with the film Tuneega Tuneega. She was cast as Nidhi in the film, opposite Sumanth Ashwin. She made her Bollywood debut the following year with Mere Dad Ki Maruti. She played Jasleen in the Indian comedy film. She received Apsara and Popular Award nominations for the part. Following the film, she had lead roles in films such as Sonali Cable, Bank Chor, and Jalebi. Aside from that, she had minor parts in films such as Half Girlfriend and Dobaara: See Your Evil. Following that, the actress will appear in the film Chehre with Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi.
