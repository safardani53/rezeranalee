Kourtney Kardashian tells Travis Barker 'til death do us part' as they dress up as Sid &amp; Nancy for H

Publish date: 2024-07-31

KOURTNEY Kardashian and her fiance Travis Barker dressed up as famed rockstar couple Sid Vicious and Nancy Spungen for their first Halloween together since their engagement.

The Blink-182 drummer proposed to Kourtney, 42, last weekend after eight months of dating.

The reality star posted a photo of their uncanny resemblance to the couple on Instagram while donning their Halloween costumes.

The series of black and white snaps featured Travis in his usual black attire, sporting a black shirt, leather jacket and dark jeans.

He completed the look with a studded choker necklace and black spikey haired wig, with a cigarette hanging out of his mouth.

Kourtney played her part by wearing black leather pants, a wide studded belt, and a black bra with a fishnet shirt.

She topped off the sexy outfit with a curly blond wig and heavy eye makeup.

The pair posed for the camera while sitting on the couch as Kourtney hooked her arm around Travis's shoulder.

In another pic, the duo are standing side by side while handcuffed to one another, mimicking the bond of the late couple.

She captioned the post: "till death do us part"

It was earlier reported that the newly-engaged couple would be posing as the punk star and his girlfriend for Halloween after Kourtney was spotted in a blonde wig dressed as Nancy sitting next to her daughter Penelope, Avril Lavigne, and her rocker boyfriend Mod Sun.

Travis joined her later but walked the red carpet dressed as Sid at the NoCap x Travis Barker House of Horrors concert on October 19 in Malibu.

He was sporting spiked black hair, an “I’m a Mess” button, and a leather jacket.

The rockstar also covered his face tattoos to look just like Sid.

Kourtney is known for her Halloween costumes from previous festivities, dressing up as Ariana Grande, a Victoria’s Secret model, and Morticia Adams in the past.

HALLOWEEN FUN

The model recently boasted that she's the "queen of Halloween" in an Instagram snap where she donned a black off-the-shoulder long-sleeve bodysuit with high black leather boots.

She posed on top of a table surrounded by Halloween decorations including sculls, skeletons and long, gothic candlesticks.

It seems that Travis, 45, also enjoys the holiday as he accompanied his fiance to a Halloween Horror Night event at Universal Studios earlier this month.

FAMILY'S EXPANDING

The outing came days before the pair's highly-anticipated engagement where Travis proposed to the star on the beach in Montecito, California.

The musician organized a lavish setting on the sand with a garden of red roses and romantic candelight surrounding them as he popped the question.

He presented the TV star with a massive oval-shaped diamond ring reportedly valued at a whopping $1 million.

The initmate moment was followed by a dinner with their families, include both Travis and Kourtney's kids.

Travis is dad to son Landon, 18 and daughter Alabama, 15 with his ex Shanna Moakler.
