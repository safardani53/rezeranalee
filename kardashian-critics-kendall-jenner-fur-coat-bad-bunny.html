Kardashian critics rip 'out of touch' Kendall Jenner as star steps out in $27k fur coat during Aspen

Publish date: 2024-02-09

KARDASHIAN critics have slammed Kendall Jenner for flaunting her wealth during her Aspen trip. 

This week, the supermodel kickstarted her snowy vacation to Aspen, Colorado. 

Kendall, 28, showed off her fancy looks while wearing knee-high boots from Rick Owens, which cost $3,000 a pair.

On Saturday night, The Kardashians star stepped out in public and modeled a hand-painted fur coat by fashion designer Phoebe Philo. 

The cost of the brown shearling comes with a hefty price tag of about $27,000.

The Hulu star loosened her dark hair and strutted in black high heels as she headed off to the sushi restaurant, Matsuhisa.

She had dinner with fellow model Lauren Perez and her husband, David Waltzer.

Kendall could not get enough of her fur coat as she took to her Instagram Stories and took a snapshot with it on. 

In an online thread, however, fans ripped the reality star in the comments section and accused her of being self-absorbed.

"Kendall's always gotta look very expensive," one person said as another remarked: "The coat is literally $27,000. What are you? A rich Manhattan housewife?"

HOLIDAY DECOR

Kendall flaunted her fur coat after showing her simple Christmas decorations at her $9 million mansion in Beverly Hills, California. 

The star took to Instagram and shared different angles of her holiday decorations in a carousel post.

Two of the sweet photos from the carousel pictured her happily posing on her couch.

The brunette model teased her bare tummy as she flexed her arms and waist.

While bending over, Kendall wore a long-sleeved navy blue top and matching leggings. 

In the background, a portion of the lit-up garland sat on the mantel mantel with short-standing Santa Claus standing next to it.

Another snap featured a clear shot of her tall Christmas tree adorned with bright string light and very few ornaments.

BREAKING UP?

Kendall's solo trip to Aspen came amid breakup rumors between her and rapper Bad Bunny, 29, also known as Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio.

Also on Friday, multiple sources claimed to The Messenger that the couple had reportedly split. 

An insider told the publication: "There's no bad blood.

"They are even still in contact. Their relationship had been rocky due to their busy schedules."

The TV personality supposedly "felt the relationship ran its course."
