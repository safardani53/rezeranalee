Posh Pawn, Bargain-Loving Brits In The Sun...check out a host of new TV shows

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Wondering what's new to watch on the box this week? Check out TV Magazine's top five new picks...

Love the high life?

Posh Pawn, Sunday, 7pm, Channel 4

James Constantinou gets his petrolhead fix this week, going to work on an E-Type Jaguar that’s worth more than £100,000. And a staggering impulse buy gets the once-over from the show’s resident handbag expert, Claudia.

Love Benidorm?

Bargain-Loving Brits In The Sun, Thursday, 8pm, Channel 5

Cold Feet’s John Thomson narrates this Benidorm-based reality show. One couple buy a caravan online that doesn’t exist, while another pair open a café doing British roasts – and face stiff competition…

Love trains?

Great American Railroad Journeys Weekdays, 6.30pm, BBC Two

Former Tory MP Michael Portillo visits America with his train-based travelogue. Starting in Boston, he becomes involved in a reconstruction of the infamous Boston Tea Party, which gave us Brits our marching orders in 1773.

Love true crime?

Robbie Coltrane’s Critical Evidence, Sunday, 10pm, C&I

Robbie returns with his award-winning show that looks at the game-changing evidence in complex murder inquiries. We begin with a Cardiff case involving groundbreaking facial reconstruction.

Love medical docs?

Inside The Ambulance, Monday, 8pm, W

More astonishing human stories and nerve-shredding action from the West Midlands Ambulance Service. The series- three opener includes a two-year-old toddler who has had a seizure and, on a lighter note, a chat about mankinis.
