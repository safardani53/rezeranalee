Publish date: 2024-02-08

ROLL UP, roll up! Sun Racing's Cheltenham Festival Preview Night is here - and it's all set to be an absolute cracker.

Hosted by none other than Matt Chapman and featuring star jockey Aidan Coleman, weighing room legend Mattie Batchelor and our very own top tipster Templegate (Steve Mullen), we've got you covered for all the big Festival winners, news and gossip.

Tickets are just £5 - buy yours HERE - and include two free drinks and snacks.

Best of all, it's really easy to get to, with the night being hosted just yards from London Bridge station on March 6.

Chappers will be your guide through the night's festivities, getting the lowdown from former Champion Chase winner Coleman.

He rides Jonbon in the Arkle - one of the most-backed horses of the week - so make sure you're there to get the very latest on Nicky Henderson's star chaser from someone who's really in the know.

And find out what he also makes of the chances of fan favourites Paisley Park and Epatante as we get inside the mind of one of the best jockeys around.

Batchelor will bring his own unique brand of comedy and unrivalled insight into the weighing room.
