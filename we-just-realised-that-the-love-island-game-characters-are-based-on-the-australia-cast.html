We just REALISED that the Love Island game characters are based on the Australia cast! Sat 11 A

Publish date: 2024-08-30

The Love Island game is pretty damn ridiculous.

However, that hasn’t stopped Love Island fanatics playing the sh*t out of a fake dating game, where you need to spend real-life money to succeed.

The aim of the game is simple: first, you create a character… who has to be female, because, of course, only girls like Love Island.

Next, you spend 1,000 hours on your iPhone flirting with boys and trying to impress them with your clothes. There’s also the case of rival females, who each come with their own crazy b*tch personality, and always attempt to steal your man.

Now, with the announcement of Love Island Australia on ITVbe, we’ve realised that the Love Island characters have some seriously uncanny similarities to the Aussie cast.

Check it out!
