Experience

Matt McNulty is a former writer at PEOPLE. He left PEOPLE in August 2019.

Before serving as a writer for PEOPLE, Matt was a content supervisor at Google from 2018 to 2019 and a content writer at Mandatory Media in 2018. He was also a staff writer for USA TODAY Sports Media Group from 2016 to 2018. Matt earned a B.A. in English at the University of Massachusetts Amherst in 2010.

About PEOPLE
