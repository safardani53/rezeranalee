Relationship Timeline Of King Bach; Who Is He Dating Now?

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Andrew Byron Bachelor, popularly known as King Bach, is an actor, comedian, and internet personality, who rose to fame through Vine videos. He is presently active on YouTube with 2.48 million subscribers and Instagram with over 20 million followers.

Since coming to the limelight, Bachelor has appeared in several mainstream movies, including Fifty Shades of Black, To All the Boys I've Loved Before, The Babysitter, and Where's the Money.

Like his professional life, the 32-year-old actor's personal life has also been in the spotlight because of his multiple relationships.

Rumored of Dating His Best Friend Amanda Cerny

King Bach was rumored of dating his best friend and internet personality Amanda Cerny. The rumors started in 2015 when they started collaborating for skits and comedy videos.

But neither of them confirmed that they were boyfriend and girlfriend.

Instead, they often mention each other as best friends on their social media. For instance, on February 9, 2020, Bachelor posted a workout video with her and captioned the post writing, "Best friend workout @AmandaCerny."

Lately, the rumors have sailed off since Cerny is dating her boyfriend Johannes Bartl, a fitness trainer.

His Dating History

In the initial days of his career, The Babysitter: Killer Queen actor was in a relationship with social media star Liane Valenzuela. They were together around the mid-2010s.

King Bach and Valenzuela were open about their relationship on social media and usually posted snaps together.

On February 26, 2015, Valenzuela took to Twitter to appreciate the actor for coming to support her at her show despite being sick.

But the pair could not take their relationship for long and broke up in 2015.

After that, there were rumors of him dating Cerny, which turned out to be a baseless assumption.

In 2016, King Bach was again linked with YouTube star Lele Pons after collaborating for a video titled 'Couple Therapy.' But the rumors were just baseless, even this time.

Lately, the comedian has been seen with actress Jessica Allain. The two even posted the same picture on their respective Instagram in late July 2020.

That being said, they are yet to confirm if they are actually dating or they are just friends. 

Is King Bach Gay?

From making parody videos about how gay men dance to a gay-themed parody trailer for the movie Get Out 2, King Bach utilized the LGBTQ+ community as a subject for his comedy. 

Although those gay-themed contents were merely for amusement, some people took the joke seriously.

While some were offended, the others started questioning whether King Bach was gay or not. But he's allegedly straight.

King Bach’s Short Bio

On June 26, 1988, King Bach was born in Toronto, Canada, to Jamaican parents, Ingrid Mourice and Byron Bachelor.

The comedian has one younger sister named Christina.

At the age of two, he and his family moved to West Palm Beach, Florida, USA. He grew up in a Christian household. He went to Coral Springs Charter School from middle to high school.

In 2010, he graduated with a degree in business management from Florida State University. After that, he enrolled at Florida State University.

Subsequently, he joined a graduate program at the New York Film Academy but dropped out during his last semester as he wanted more options in life.

He then moved to Los Angeles and studied improvisational theatre at The Groundlings.
