Jennifer Lopez Visits Easter Bunny with Emme and Max: PHOTOS

Publish date: 2024-05-18

Jennifer Lopez and her boyfriend Casper Smart took her twins Emme and Max to visit the Easter Bunny House at the Grove in Hollywood on Thursday.

The four-year-old children of JLo and Marc Anthony posed with the big rabbit as their mother joined them for the photo op.

Young Emme carried a stuffed animal and her brother Max, who bears a striking resemblance to his father, clutched a blanket.

Last night Jennifer rocked a 50-carat yellow radiant diamond ring set in 18k yellow gold and ebony wood. The mega gem is reportedly worth $2.124,780 according to Cora Jewelry CEO.

Suzette Gomes said, “Having someone like Jennifer Lopez wear our ring is the ultimate. She represents a strong woman with a great sense of work ethic and intelligence. She is the American dream and now, our American jewelry idol, too.”
