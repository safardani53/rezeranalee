Gemini Syndrome is an American alternative metal band formed in 2010 in Los Angeles, California. The band currently consists of vocalist Aaron Nordstrom, drummer Brian Steele Medina and bassist Alessandro "" Paveri. Their debut album, titled Lux, was released in September 2013. Gemini Syndrome released their sophomore album, Memento Mori, on August 16, 2016, which hit #1 on Billboard Heatseakers Charts Five singles spawned from Memento Mori: "Eternity,""Anonymous," "Alive Inside,"" "Sorry Not Sorry," and "Remember We Die," which reached no. 24, and spent 20 weeks on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart.
