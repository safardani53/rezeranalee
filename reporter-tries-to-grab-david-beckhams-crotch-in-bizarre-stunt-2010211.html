A female Italian reporter wearing yellow gloves attempted to molest David Beckham's genitals in a shocking publicity stunt on Wednesday. "We have seen fascinating photographs of David Beckham in his underpants," Di Cioccio told her viewers, referencing his famous underwear ads for Armani.
