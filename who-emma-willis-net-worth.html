What is Emma Willis age and net worth? The Sun

Publish date: 2024-08-31

OVER the years Emma Willis has become a household name, thanks to her impressive career.

But before becoming a TV presenter, she started life as a model.

Who is Emma Willis and what is her age?

Emma Willis was born in Sutton Coldfield in Birmingham on March 18, 1976.

Her first venture into media was at age 15 when she started modelling.

However, she later revealed how she was fat-shamed as a teen model, being told she was too fat by an agent.

She modelled for hugely successful companies such as Marie Claire, Elle, Vogue, GAP and Chanel.

Despite the awful treatment as a young woman, she says that those experiences help her to deal with the online trolls she faces now.

Emma took to radio and presenting and became one of the leading TV presenters in the country.

She caught her big break in 2002 when she landed a presenting role with MTV.

What TV shows has Emma Willis hosted?

In 2002, Emma began her first big presenting job with MTV and after that, she went on to guest host CD:UK, This Morning and Loose Women.

By 2014, Emma had made a name for herself in the television world having co-presented shows such as the ITV2 spin-off series I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! NOW!.

She then replaced Brian Dowling as the presenter of Big Brother after producers believed she was the real successor to Davina McCall.

In 2015, Emma was announced as the host of The Voice UK and The Voice Kids.

Emma has been chosen to present at many award shows, including co-presenting the Brit Awards in 2017 when Michael Buble was forced to withdraw after his young son became ill.

During Easter in 2018, Emma and her Big Brother co-host Rylan Clark-Neal stood in for Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield on Good Morning while they had a well-deserved break.

In October 2018 she trained at Princess Alexandra Hospital's busy maternity ward for a show called Delivering Babies on the W Channel.

In the third series of the show in 2021, Emma, who was by then a fully qualified Maternity Care Assistant, shared the experiences of families who had their birthing plans and lives disrupted by the Covid-19 pandemic.

Another of her shows The Circle,  also returned in 2021.

In 2021, she hosted Cooking With The Stars, along with comedian Tom Allen.

Emma also teamed up with Nick Grimshaw to host The Great Home Transformation.

Adding another string to her bow, she took part in Celebrity Bake Off - and won.

Emma can also be found hosting This Morning from time to time.

What is Emma Willis' net worth?

Both Emma and her husband Matt Willis are successful in their own right.

Matt was part of the hugely popular British band Busted.

After years of success on TV and radio shows, Emma is worth a pretty penny also.

Emma is worth an estimated £6.5million.

Who is Emma Willis' husband Matt Willis and do they have children?

Emma met husband Matt in the early 2000s and they started dating in 2005.

After three years of dating, they tied the knot in a beautiful ceremony at Rushton Hall, Northamptonshire, on July 5, 2008.

To celebrate their 10th wedding anniversary in July 2018 they renewed their vows.

Emma wore her original bridal dress and her star guests, including Rochelle Humes, also wore their own white gowns.

Matt and Emma have three children together - Ace, Isabelle and Trixie Grace.

Matt is an actor, singer and presenter.

Most will recognise him as part of the iconic rock trio, Busted, along with James Bourne, and Charlie Simpson.

Busted hits include Year 3000, What I Go To School For, Sleeping With The Light On and Air Hostess.

They had four UK No1 hits with You Said No, Crashed The Wedding, Who's David and double A single Thunderbirds/3am

After the band split, James and Matt joined McFly to create McBusted, who toured between 2013 and 2015.

The newly formed band had a No12 hit with Air Guitar in 2014.

Busted reunited in 2016 releasing albums Night Driver (2016) and Half Way There (2019).

Matt also appeared on our TV screens in 2006, as viewers voted for him to become King of the Jungle on I'm A Celebrity.

His win saw him host the I'm A Celeb spin-off in 2007, alongside wife Emma.
