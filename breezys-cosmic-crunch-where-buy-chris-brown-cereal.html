Breezy's Cosmic Crunch: Where can I buy Chris Brown's cereal?

Publish date: 2024-04-19

CHRIS Brown has done a lot of things throughout his career, and he is now adding cereal connoisseur to his resume.

In April 2022, he announced a new collaboration with Miami-based retailer SoFlo Snacks, and now many want to know where they can buy his cereal.

Breezy's Cosmic Crunch: Where can I buy Chris Brown's cereal?

Breezy’s Cosmic Crunch is a new breakfast cereal made with galactic marshmallow charms and sweetened oats.

The cereal was created in partnership with Brown and comes in a collectible box with unique QR codes that provide the chance to win exclusive prizes, Billboard reports.

“Chris Brown has been an icon in the music industry for decades, and he has been a major part of the whole process in crafting Breezy’s Cosmic Crunch cereal," SoFlo snacks operator Jonathan Rodriguez said in a statement.

 “His fans are some of the most dedicated in the world, we expect it to sell out quite quickly.”

The limited-edition treat is available for purchase online, starting at $23.99.

What has Chris Brown said about the collaboration?

Following the release of the cereal, Brown talked about his excitement for the product and the opportunity to meet fans.

more from Chris brown

“It’s been exciting creating Breezy’s Cosmic Crunch, I wanted to create something my fans could enjoy while also giving them something they could keep and collect forever,” Brown said.

“I’m also hyped to meet with some of my fans. Who will win the video call?!”

Along with a video call, fans also have the opportunity to win autographed boxes, gift cards, and more.

Prior to SoFlo's collaboration with Brown, the company partnered with Lil Durk and Druski.

What is Chris Brown's net worth?

Brown stepped into the entertainment industry back in 2005 and has since released nine studio albums.

As of April 2022, his net worth is estimated to be around $50million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Outside of music, Brown has also dabbled in acting and has been seen in Takers, Think Like a Man, Battle of the Year, and Black-ish.
