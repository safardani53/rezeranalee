Oliver Hudson Baby: Goldie Hawn's Son Welcomes Daughter Rio

Publish date: 2024-10-06

Goldie's a grandma — again! Goldie Hawn's eldest son, actor Oliver Hudson, has welcomed a little girl named Rio with wife Erinn Bartlett, the 67-year-old actress announced on Twitter.

"Welcome Rio! Our first baby girl to join our family!" she tweeted on Thursday, July 18. "@theoliverhudson and Erinn, thank you for bringing so much JOY to our lives! #happiness."

Rio is Hudson and Bartlett's third child together and only daughter. Married since 2006, the couple are also parents to two sons, Wilder, 5, and Bodhi, 3.
