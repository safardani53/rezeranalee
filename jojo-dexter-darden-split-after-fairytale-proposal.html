JoJo and fianc Dexter Darden split 1 year after fairytale proposal as singer shares cryptic post a

Publish date: 2024-05-03

JoJo and her fiancé Dexter Darden have split one year after the actor's fairytale proposal, The U.S. Sun can exclusively report.

The Leave (Get Out) singer announced her engagement on Christmas in 2021, a few days after the Saved by the Bell star proposed on her birthday at a luxury resort in Puerto Rico.

A source confirms to The U.S. Sun that the pair broke off their engagement in November 2022.

They have since unfollowed each other on Instagram, and JoJo has removed photos of them together, including her engagement announcement.

ALL THE SIGNS

The 32-year-old songstress has posted cryptic messages on her social media in recent weeks, leaving fans to suspect that the couple had ended their relationship.

JoJo shared on a recent Instagram Story: "And just like that, I took the ring off my left hand and put it on my right."

The star also quoted Jay Shetty, former monk and life coach, in another cryptic message: "The right person won't just love you with their words. They will love you with their intentions, actions, and affection.

"They will love all of you with all they have."

In addition, she posted a TikTok about "grieving" while trying to relax in a bubble bath.

She captioned the video: "Seriously there is no one size fits all to grieving/ healing/ being pissed tf off."

Fans even speculated that Dexter, 31, had cheated, after she posted messages like: "People be lying, knowing they lying, knowing you know they lying...And still lie."

The U.S. Sun has reached out to JoJo and Dexter's reps for comment.

HOLIDAYS APART

The end of 2022 looked a bit different for the platinum-selling pop star.

For her December 20 birthday, she posted a photo of herself clearly not wearing her engagement ring.

She captioned the smiling pic: "Endlessly blessed. Thank you for all the birthday wishes ilsym I can’t even begin to explain.

"Thank you thank you thank you God for another year."

A few days later, she spent Christmas with her family in her hometown of Foxborough, Massachusetts, with her ex-fiancé noticeably absent from her posts.

Then, in her end-of-the-year round-up, she posted that there were "so many phenomenal highs and a couple significant lows.

"22 came through- and shook things up in ways I wasn't quite expecting.

"You showed me how to not resist the way things are and to not put my sense of security in any outside person or institution or timeline."

MOVING ON

JoJo is starting fresh in the new year.

On January 2, she posted that she moved into a "new place" and was excited about decorating.

She asked fans for artists recommendations for art to display on her walls.

Meanwhile, Dexter has been promoting his projects like Hulu's The Bing 2: It's A Wonderful Binge.

He also posted a motivational gym pic in December with the caption: "Best part about getting a workout in when you’re Down, is the lift your spirit gets when the workouts done.

"Thank you God, for bringing the extra 'little lift' I needed this week. Death, and grief can feel so final, but New Life brings New Hope!"

The former couple had been together since June 2020.

JoJo's engagement announcement at the time stated: "forever with you? Sign me up. celebrating Christmas a whole fiancé!!! The most thoughtful, creative, positive, handsome, strong, loving, uplifting human being asked me to marry him. so obviously I said yes!
