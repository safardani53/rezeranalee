Amal and George Clooney Arrive at the Royal Wedding: Pic

Publish date: 2024-05-09

An image of perfection! Amal Clooney was a sight to see as she and husband George Clooney arrived at Windsor Castle for Prince Harry and Meghan Markle’s nuptials.

Amal, 40, turned heads in a canary yellow Prada gown. A source previously revealed to Us Weekly that the human rights attorney had her last dress fitting on Tuesday, May 15.

The barrister’s stylist, Miguel Perez, told HOLA! magazine last month that he would be styling her for the regal occasion and planned to “leave [her hair] loose for the ceremony and then do an up-do for the party.”

Perez will also be in charge of the bride-to-be’s hair, after Amal recommended him to the future royal.

The A-list couple are two of many celebrities attending the royal wedding, including the Spice Girls, Serena Williams, Priyanka Chopra, the Suits cast and more.

Although it is unclear if the Clooneys will attend the intimate evening reception at Frogmore House following the wedding and luncheon, a source exclusively told Us Weekly that Markle’s former costars will not be joining the newlyweds at the nighttime bash.
