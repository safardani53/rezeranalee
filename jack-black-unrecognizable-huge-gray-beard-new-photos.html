Publish date: 2024-08-17

JACK Black looked unrecognizable with a huge gray beard while taking a walk in Los Angeles in new photos.

The actor has starred in a slew of Blockbuster films becoming one of Hollywood's most successful comedic stars.

The 52-year-old shocked fans with his appearance during a recent outing sporting long facial hair and sunglasses.

Jack quickly covered up with a face mask after spotting cameras while donning a red wolf tee-shirt, black shorts, and sneakers.

The musician's look differed from how fan's usually see him on-screen with a shorter beard and hairstyle.

Jack - who is also a voice actor, director, and producer - has appeared in hit movies including School Of Rock and The Holiday over his nearly 30 year career.

He broke out into the Hollywood scene in the 2000 film High Fidelity. 

The comedian's career then took off leading to roles in popular film's Gulliver’s Travels and Jumanji, among others.
