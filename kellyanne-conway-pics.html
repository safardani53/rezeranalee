Pics Of Donald Trumps White House Counselor Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Kellyanne Conway, who was born on Jan. 20, 1967, works as a counselor to the president in Donald Trump’s administration. She began her work with the administration by serving as Trump’s campaign manager during the 2016 election. She took on this role in August 2016, and helped to get Trump elected that November. After he was elected as president, Kellyanne officially became one of his counselor’s in Dec. 2016. Kellyanne previously lived in Trump Towers from 2001-2008, and she worked with Trump in 2013 when he was considering a run for Governor of New York. Kellyanne has been married to lawyer, George Conway, since 1998, and they have four children together. George and Trump have very different views and have publicly bashed each other on more than one occasion.
