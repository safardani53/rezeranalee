Sachin Tyagi Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Sachin Tyagi has worked in many advertisements, movies, and TV Commercials. He has worked in a number of television shows like ‘Solaah Singgar’, ‘Kaajjal’, ‘Tere Shehar Main’, ‘The Adventures of Hatim’, ‘Tumhari Disha’. He was firstly considered for the role of ‘Swayam’ in ‘Kahin To Hoga‘ which was aired on Star Plus but he dropped later by producer Ekta Kapoor for some personal issues. He was nominated for Star Guild Award for the Best Actor in Supporting Role. He was last seen  Swaragini which is being telecast on Colors Channel. 

Sachin Tyagi belongs to south Asian ethnicity and holds an Indian nationality. He is married to Rakshanda Khan who is a model, actress, and anchor in the Indian Television Industry. The couples met on the sets of popular reality show ‘Kabhi Kabhii Pyaar Kabhi Kabhii Yaar‘ which was directed by Ekta Kapoor. This beautiful couple got married on 15 March 2014 in Mumbai and blessed with three children named  Pari, Saadhvi, Enaya Tyagi.

Sachin earns a good amount of salary from his acting career which is reported to around IRS 25-350,000 per episode which add up to his current estimated net worth of around $ 1 million.
