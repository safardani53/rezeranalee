Gary Woodland has an elder sister named CJ Woodland Head, who has a corporate job in the marketing field.

Gary Woodland is an American professional golfer who has won 4 PGA Tour titles.

His first major championship was his US Open win in 2019, which moved his World Golf Ranking from 25th to 12th, his career-highest ranking.

The professional golfer attended Shawnee Heights High School and later enrolled at Washburn University on a basketball scholarship.

However, after a year, he left for the University of Kansas in Lawrence on a golf scholarship.

He had a successful collegiate golf career. As a member of the Kansas Jayhawks, he won four tournaments before turning professional in 2007.

Woodland played in a handful of tournaments on the Nationwide. In 2008, he entered the Qualifying school for the PGA Tour and earned a full card to play on the PGA Tour after finishing in a tie for 11th.

The American golfer won his first PGA Tour title at the Transitions Championship, beating Webb Simpson.

Gary Woodland Family
