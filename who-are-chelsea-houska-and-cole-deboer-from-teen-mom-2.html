Who are Chelsea Houska And Cole DeBoer From Teen Mom 2?

Publish date: 2024-02-03

The couple from Teen Mom 2, Chelsea Houska, and Cole DeBoer, have a lot on their plates. Aside from balancing four children and a hectic schedule, the two have landed their program. Chelsea Houska and Cole DeBoer will play enthusiastic renovators on HGTV’s Farmhouse Fabulous, restoring the houses of hopeful families. The pair are gearing up to begin filming next month, with the home improvement program set to broadcast in spring 2023.

Who are Teen Mom 2’s Chelsea Houska and Cole DeBoer?

Chelsea Houska, a South Dakota native, came to stardom in 2011 with the program Teen Mom 2, a show about adolescent females who get pregnant. Houska and her ex-boyfriend Adam reared their daughter Aubree. When Chelsea met Cole DeBoer at a petrol station in 2014, everything changed. They then spoke over social media and got along well. The pair married in 2015 and have four children together: Aubree, Watson, Layne, and Walker.

The MTV pair appeared on Teen Mom 2 from season 6 through season 10 and earned a lot of attention for their heartwarming tale. However, in 2020, they chose to leave the program and concentrate on their personal life. Currently, the duo works as enthusiastic house renovators under Home DeBoers. The MTV couple has lovingly repaired a number of homes. Chelsea Houska also owns and operates a few more enterprises. Aubree Says’ shop provides home-decorating supplies. Houska and her pals own Belle & Rae Co, which creates lightroom presets for influencers.

Chelsea revealed in a press release about making a TV return with a home renovation program two years after leaving Teen Mom 2, “After creating our ideal farmhouse in Sioux Falls a few years ago, we discovered we had a great talent for design and home renovation, and it’s blossomed into our passion.”  Dale DeBoer, the couple’s fur baby, also has his own Instagram profile, where they share cute images of him and his everyday activities.
