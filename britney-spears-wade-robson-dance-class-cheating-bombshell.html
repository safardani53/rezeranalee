BRITNEY Spears' fling, Wade Robson, has wasted no time in getting down to business after the pop star's bombshell cheating claims.

Shared to his Instagram Stories, Wade posted videos of him leading a dance class at the RADIX Dance Competition in Miami, Florida, over the weekend.

Robson was there to teach classes as a member of the Radix faculty, who tour the United States giving workshops.

While there, he taught several teens a few dance moves.

On Saturday, October 21, Wade was spotted for the first time in Miami since the bombshell news dropped.

It comes after The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed earlier this week that Spears finally confessed to their fling following years of rumors in her upcoming memoir The Woman in Me - out on October 24.

He rocked a fuchsia top, gray sweatpants, and bright orange activewear shoes as he arrived at the Miami Beach Convention Center.

BRIT'S CLAIMS

Gossip has swirled for years that Britney, 41, split from Justin in 2002 after getting with Wade.

It has long been assumed that Justin’s 2002 hit Cry Me A River is about Britney and Wade’s alleged affair.

In her memoir, Britney claims she cheated one time with Wade but only after finding out that Justin had slept around behind her back.

She described her alleged fling with Wade: “We were out one night and we went to a Spanish bar. We danced and danced. I made out with him that night.”

She goes on to say that she had been loyal to Justin for years “with that one exception."

Britney also says she confessed the affair to Justin, but they were able to move past it and stay together.

WHAT OTHERS SAY

Songwriter Annet Artani - who helped write Britney’s 2003 song Everytime - told TMZ that she thinks Britney's romance with Wade was much deeper than what she had claimed.

Artani revealed that Justin found a 14-page letter that Britney had written for Wade.

Allegedly, she left it in her bag and when he read it, he subsequently broke up with her.  

Artani said: “I don’t think it was making out in a bar one night. I remember specifically hearing her say, I mean she told me this story, how it was with Wade and it went on for quite a while and she wrote him this long letter, it was like 14 pages and it was in her makeup bag.

"To my understanding, if you are writing someone a 14-page letter I would think it’s more than just 'We kissed one time.'

Recorded in November 2002, Britney’s track Everytime has long been assumed to have been her response to Justin’s digs at her in Cry Me A River."

'HEART-WRENCHING DECISIONS'

The revelations about Britney’s fling with Robson came after it emerged that Britney had an abortion after she got pregnant with Justin’s baby in around 2000 when the pair were 19.

Following “heart-wrenching discussions” the former couple - who dated from 1999 to 2002 - agreed not to go ahead with the pregnancy because they were too young.
