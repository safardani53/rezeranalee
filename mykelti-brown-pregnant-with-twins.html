Mykelti Brown: Pregnant! With TWINS!

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Mykelti Brown is about to be a mother.

Once again.

Times two this time around.

Meaning what, exactly?

The Sister Wives star is pregnant! With twins!

In blessed news broken by People Magazine, Mykelti announced on Thursday that she and her husband, Tony, are expecting another pair of children.

(They’re already parents to a 14-month old daughter named Avalon.)

“I’m extremely excited for the twins to come,” Mykelti tells People.

“Nervous as well especially for the first 18 months of their lives, but it will be a wonderful adventure.”

On Instagram, meanwhile, Brown linked to this outlet’s coverage of her ongoing pregnancy and added:

WE’RE HAVING TWINS !!!! Miss Avalon will be a big sister in December (twins come earlier so probably before then).

Tony and I definitely weren’t planning this or expecting it but we’re both excited for our upcoming adventure and all the chaos and love it brings

T#people.com #expectingtwins #17weekspregnant #bigsister.

Tony also jumped in as follows, stating simply:

“I’m excited about having double the trouble and double the fun.”

Mykelti is the daughter of Kody and Christine Brown, the latter of whom walked out of her marriage in November 2021.

These new additions, meanwhile, will be Kody’s fourth and fifth grandchildren — as daughter Maddie Brown Brush is mom to daughter Evangalynn Kodi, 2, and son Axel James, 4, whom she shares with husband Caleb Brush.

As cited above, Christine Brown now lives in her native state of Utah after leaving Kody several months ago.

This means she’ll be able to help with the impending children, however.

“I’m so excited, they’re going to be such great parents,” Christine told People.

“I know they’re a bit overwhelmed because it’s twins, it’s a lot of work but I live close, Aspyn lives close.”

Mykelti and Tony welcomed their first child together on April 5, 2021.

They got married in December 2016 in front of over 400 guests at the Bloomington Country Club in St. George, Utah.

“She’s got such an amazing support system, we’re all so excited,” concluded Christine this week to People.

“They’ve been incredible parents with Avalon and now we get to see them be parents again!”
