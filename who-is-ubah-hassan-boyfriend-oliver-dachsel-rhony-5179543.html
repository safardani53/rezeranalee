Who Is RHONY Star Ubah Hassans Boyfriend? Meet Oliver Dachsel Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-03-04

The Real Housewives of New York City star Ubah Hassan is done keeping her relationship with “Mr. Connecticut” a secret. After hiding her love life from the cameras which led to major drama at the season 14 finale, the 40-year-old model debuted her handsome boyfriend, Oliver Dachsel, during their first red carpet appearance together on October 24. Ubah and Oliver looked so in love as they posed for pictures at the 2023 American Ballet Theatre Fall Gala at the Lincoln Center in New York City. Ubah’s secret boyfriend is a secret no more!

So, who is Oliver Dachsel? Get to know the successful businessman who is dating one of the new stars of the RHONY reboot.

Oliver Dachsel is an investment banker.

Oliver currently works as the managing director of the investment banking company Jeffries, according to his LinkedIn. He was previously the senior vice president at the company, which is based out of New York. Before he started working at Jefferies in August 2016, Oliver was at Deutsche Bank for over ten years.

Oliver went to Columbia.

Oliver attended Columbia Business School in New York. He was part of the executive program at the Ivy League business school.

Oliver served in the German Army.

Oliver, who is German, bravely served his country by joining the German Federal Armed Forces. He served in the Mountain Ranger Battalion 232 of the German Armed Forces from August 2001 to June 2002, according to his LinkedIn.

Oliver lives in Connecticut.

Oliver was dubbed “Mr. Connecticut” on RHONY. Ubah kept their relationship off the show and only told Sai De Silva that she was dating someone who lives in Connecticut. But when Sai spilled the beans, drama ensued…

Oliver was a source of drama on RHONY.

During the RHONY season 14 finale, it was revealed that Sai told Erin Lichy and Brynn Whitfield about Ubah’s relationship with “Mr. Connecticut” off-camera. Brynn exposed Ubah’s secret at her birthday party, causing a big fight between the entire cast. Ubah stormed out of the party and refused to share anything about her love life.

However, Ubah finally opened up a little more about her relationship on the October 8 episode of Watch What Happens Live.

“I think I kept it a secret because I’m so tired because every time I meet someone, I want everyone to see. Like my dog and everyone, and if it doesn’t work, then I have to go back and tell everyone it doesn’t work,” she told Andy Cohen. “I’m so tired [of saying], ‘Oh, it doesn’t work anymore.’ ” Ubah also said on WWHl that she and Oliver have been together “a really long time.”
