Mum-of-22 Sue Radford assures fans amid hurricane warning as family share peek inside Magic Kingdom

Publish date: 2024-03-13

MUM-OF-22 Sue Radford has taken her family to a theme park amid warnings of a hurricane nearing Florida. 

Hurricane Idalia has already hit Florida’s Cedar Key and is forecasted to reach a Category 4 intensity. 

But Sue has assured fans that they’re safe for the moment as she took to her social media platform to share: “Lots of messages about the hurricane so far we are all ok but keeping a close eye on the news for updates,” followed by a praying hands emoji. 

In fact, it appears the family are not too bothered at all as they’ve even gone to enjoy a day out at Magic Kingdom Park, which is a Walt Disney World Resort. 

Sharing scenes from their time at the theme park, she posted a clip of the area by a lake, where viewers could see some dark clouds overhead. 

“So excited for Magic Kingdom,” she wrote over this as they approached the park on a ferry.

She then posted a photo of her wrist, which had a band that said “not so scary Halloween Party”. 

Posting a series of photos from the park, viewers could see that the place had been decked out with autumnal decorations. 

The park even has lamp posts with orange pumpkin-shaped Mickey Mouse sculptures alongside orange, red and yellow leaves in a wreath around them. 

She then reshared a photo of her kids all posing with wide smiles plastered across their faces as they stood in front of a large castle.

In another scene, the family visited an indoor installation where there were neon platforms with dolls on them. 

Sue also couldn’t help but post a sweet photo of her child looking over the moon with the trip to Magic Kingdom. 

“The face of pure joy,” she wrote over this with a smiling emoji. 

She then showed off an incredible toy they picked up, which was designed in the theme of The Nightmare Before Christmas.

The Mayor from the film could be seen driving a toy car while Shock appeared in the back seat and Lock poked his head out the side. 

And, clearly having a grand time, another clip showed her kids dancing and having fun as they collected what appeared to be sweets off the ground and placed them into their bags. 

This isn’t the first theme park they’ve visited since arriving in the US as they’ve also gone to Universal Studios and enjoyed the waterslides. 

And though they seem to be loving every moment of their 18th holiday in just 20 months, it hasn’t all been plain sailing. 

Just days ago, the family had to rush to the emergency room for the second time as Sue told her followers: “Second time here in a few days.

“First Daisy with a nasty ear infection, now it’s Connor’s turn.” 

Sue and Noel are parents to Chris, 34, Sophie, 29, Chloe, 28, Jack, 26, Daniel, 24, Luke, 22, Millie, 21, Katie, 20, James, 19, Ellie, 18, Aimee, 17, Josh, 16, Max, 14, Tillie, 13, Oscar, 11, Casper, 10, Hallie, eight, Phoebe, seven, Archie, five, Bonnie, four, and Heidie, three.

Sadly the couple's 17th child Alfie was still born on July 6, 2014.

Daisy is the daughter of Sophie and her husband Joe Broadley, and is Sue and Noel’s oldest grandchild.

Connor is Kate’s boyfriend, who has joined them on the family holiday.
