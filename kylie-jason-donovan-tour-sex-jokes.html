Friends say Kylie could go cold on ex Jason Donovan after his crude jokes about their sex life on hi

Publish date: 2024-03-09

KYLIE MINOGUE and Jason Donovan were the most iconic Aussie duo of the Eighties – but their relationship appears to be going Down Under.

Pals of Kylie’s fear that things will become strained between the Neighbours co-stars and former lovers, who split in 1988, after Jason made a series of gags at the expense of the pop princess.

On his Amazing Midlife Crisis UK Tour, Jason — who was Neighbours’ Scott Robinson to Kylie’s Charlene Mitchell — recounted tales of his sex life with Kylie and his struggle to get her to sing with him.

A source told us: “As well as singing some of his most famous songs, Jason also shares with the audience stories about his career.

“When asked whether there was a lot of Jason in the character of Scott Robinson, he cheekily replied that there was certainly a lot of Scott in Charlene.”

And despite an impromptu performance of their 1988 hit, Especially For You, for Kylie’s headline slot at Radio 2 Live in Hyde Park last month, Jason said that she “hogged” the microphone.

The source added: “He told a tale about how he tried to get Kylie to join him on stage at London’s Hammersmith Apollo and attempted to contact her.

"His emails bounced back, his WhatsApp messages were ignored and he couldn’t reach her on Instagram.

“Then he was in the audience at Hyde Park last month and Kylie’s manager asked him on stage at the last minute, so it wasn’t pre-planned.

"He said that’s why he didn’t sing during the track and talked about Kylie hogging the mic.

“The stories have started filtering back to Kylie through her friends — and her close circle don’t think she will be best pleased.

"It’s always disappointing to hear someone is making jokes at your expense.

“As far as Kylie is concerned, Jason is a great guy and she respects him, she’s just a busy lady.”

Last night, a rep for Kylie declined to make a comment.

On Friday night, Kylie took to the stage for her third sold-out show at London’s O2 Arena — and opted to sing Especially For You by herself.

Perhaps Jason was busy writing more emails.
