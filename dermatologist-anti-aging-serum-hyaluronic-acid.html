Im a dermatologist &amp; have tested hundreds of anti-aging face serums here are 5 that actually

Publish date: 2024-08-08

YOU can't stop the aging process, but you can keep your skin looking great year after year.

One dermatologist said there are five anti-aging serums she loves best out of the hundreds she's tested, and they all share a common ingredient.

"Over the years, I've been lucky enough to try almost every type of anti-aging technique and product out there," explained Dr Gabriela Vee, a dermatologist who's sampled hundreds of products with over 500 clients.

Writing for The Bare Beauty Babes, Dr Vee said that one topical ingredient, in particular, has impressed her over and over again when it comes to preventing fine lines and maintaining skin texture.

Hyaluronic acid is Dr Vee's favorite ingredient, and she said serums deliver the most impressive anti-aging results.

"Molecules in serums are smaller than in creams & lotions," she explained, "allowing clinically active ingredients to penetrate deeper into the skin."

If you're looking for a budget serum to start your anti-aging journey, try the CeraVe Hydrating Hyaluronic Acid Serum, $23.

"This trusted brand contains gentle ingredients that are better for sensitive skin, at a reasonable price," Dr Vee said.

Despite the inexpensive price tag, CeraVe's serum is packed full of other ingredients, like vitamin B5 and ceramides, to help reduce the appearance of fine lines.

While you shouldn't expect pro-level results from the serum, it's worth using if you want to save money while taking care of your skin, Dr Vee explained.

If you want a plumping effect, the super-hydrating Water Drench Hyaluronic Cloud Serum by Peter Thomas Roth is a great option at $68. The thick gel will add moisture to the skin and leave you looking visibly younger.

"This product has a very high amount of hyaluronic acid (75 percent) in addition to silk proteins, zinc, copper, manganese, iron and silicon to increase moisture levels and soften skin," Dr Vee said.

It's best used as a night cream since it has a bit of a sticky finish, she added.

Dr Vee said her personal favorite serum is the Renew Resurfacing Night Serum from the brand Naked & Thriving. Priced at $68, the serum was so effective it even took the dermatologist by surprise.

"This serum blew me away," Dr Vee said. "The anti-aging effects came within just one week of daily use whereas no other serum worked faster."

It's so effective that she doesn't just recommend it to clients: this is Dr Vee's serum of choice for personal use, too.

You can apply this serum and then go about your day thanks to the unique formula that allows super-fast absorption.

What if you're not just worried about wrinkles, but battling other signs of aging, too, like discoloration and severely dry patches?

If that describes your skin, Dr Vee recommends using the super-powerful SkinCeuticals Hyaluronic Acid Intensifier, $106.

"This hardworking serum is pricey," she admitted, "but is highly effective at restoring parched skin."

Plus, the ingredients aim to battle a full list of skin concerns. Licorice root and purple rice, for example, reduce common skin issues like hyperpigmentation.

Finally, if you have "sandpaper-dry" skin that's resisted other treatments, Dr Vee said the SkinMedica HA5 Hydrating Complex is your best bet for serious anti-aging results.

"This satisfyingly rich serum could be a serious game-changer for you," she said. At $111, the serum is the highest-priced recommendation on Dr Vee's list, but with good reason.

The formula is enriched with not one, but five different types of hyaluronic acid. Those complexes work together with peptides to help speed up collagen production, Dr Vee explained.

"It leaves the skin looking plump and smooth immediately," she explained, "while also working to stave off fine lines in the long run."
