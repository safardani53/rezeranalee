Jack Osbourne and his wife, Lisa Stelly, seemed like a perfect match. The couple, who date for quite some time, took their relationship completely to the next level in 2012 when Lisa gave birth to their first child, a daughter named Pearl Clementine Osbourne, born on April 24. It wasn’t long after the couple welcomed their first daughter that they finally exchanged ‘I dos’ on October 7, 2012 in Hawaii. Although the couple were so happy in their newly married lives, they were hit with tragedy in 2013. The couple shared that Lisa suffered a miscarriage of their son, who would have been named Theo, during her second trimester. A few years later, however the couple welcomed their second daughter, Andy Rose Osbourne, on June 13, 2015! Finally the couple welcomed their third daughter, Minnie Theodora Osbourne, on February 3, 2018. The couple announced their separation on 18 May 2018 and their divorce was finalized on March 5, 2019.
