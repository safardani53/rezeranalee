Would You Get Plastic Surgery With Your Mom, Like OC Housewife Lynne Curtin? Hollywood Lif

Publish date: 2024-06-14

The Real Housewives of O.C.‘s Lynne Curtin and her teen daughter Raquel had some mother-daughter bonding…by going under the knife together

Talk about an intense bonding experience! Last summer, The Real Housewives of Orange County‘s Lynne Curtin got plastic surgery with her 19-year-old daughter Raquel. One eye-lift and a nose job later, the mother-daughter duo are finally dishing about their transforming experiences.

Lynne is more than aware that getting surgery with her daughter was a controversial move, but she still thinks it was a worthwhile experience.

“It’s not radical, and it makes her feel good,” Lynne told Life & Style Weekly. “I’m happy that she’s happy. And I’m also happy because I don’t have to listen to her complain about it anymore!”

Raquel had her mom’s blessing, but she says she would have gotten the work done whether her mom approved or not. “I really wanted it, and if she’d said no, I might have gone to a terrible doctor,” Raquel said. “But she was supportive and took me to an amazing surgeon. It was really cool of her.”

Did the experience make them closer? Lynne thinks so! “It was just a total mother-daughter experience,” she raved. “Recovering together was cool. We got each other ice packs and ordered takeout.”

We have to admit, getting tandem plastic surgery with your daughter totally fits into The Real Housewives image! It’s a little more expensive than shopping and a lot more intense than seeing a movie…
