Crafty mum gives her bathroom a makeover for just 30 using chic grey paint and marble Fablon

Publish date: 2024-08-08

A MUM has revealed how she transformed her drab bathroom into a chic new space for just £30.

Taking to the Facebook group, DIY on a Budget Official she shared the amazing before and after photos of the room.

Before its transformation the bathroom was seriously lacking in style decorated with beige tiles and a classic cream three-piece suite.

However, the mum’s bathroom looks completely different in shades of grey – décor that Mrs Hinch would be proud of.

She used a £20 tile paint from B&Q to cover her dated splash back, and then coated the side of the bath in a marble effect Fablon – making it look like a whole new suite.

Captioning the post she wrote: “I couldn’t afford to change my tiles and wanted to make it my own so did a little revamp… cost £30 in total… and so pleased with the result. Just need to get a new floor, but here’s it so far.

“Got to add the accessories yet… but wanted to show the painted tiles and Fablon bath panel up cycles for those on a budget.

“I didn’t like the beige… I love grey… all 50 shades of it!”

And fellow group members were seriously impressed with the post receiving over 5,000 likes and users praising the mum’s handiwork in the comments.

One wrote: “Wow this is a huge improvement!”

“It looks amazing, what a good idea” said another, while a third wrote, “I want and need this.”
