LeAnn Rimes, Bikini-Clad Friends Recreate Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue Cover

Publish date: 2024-01-30

The 2014 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue cover, featuring Chrissy Teigen, Lily Aldridge and Nina Agdal, was hailed as a hit across the nation.

LeAnn Rimes was apparently a big fan. The singer showed off her own amazing figure when she posted a cheeky bikini photo via Twitter on Monday.

Captioned “Models in training @SInow sequel #happymemorialdayboys #wetryhard #having fun,” Rimes and two pals created a lighthearted remake.

With LeAnn Rimes and Eddie Cibrian‘s VH1 reality show set to begin soon, she is all smiles, and with good reason. At least when it comes to her figure.

Clearly, she’s been working out and proud of it!

“Workouts done!” Rimes tweeted to trainer Ilyse Baker earlier in the day. “Every inch of me is drenched!! @ilysebaker time for sun & a bikini!”
