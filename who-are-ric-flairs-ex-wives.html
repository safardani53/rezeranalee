Who are Ric Flair's ex-wives?

Publish date: 2024-05-30

PROFESSIONAL wrestler Ric Flair has been married five times in his life.

Richard Morgan Fliehr's impressive wrestling career has spanned over 40 years.

Who are Ric Flair's ex-wives?

Leslie Goodman

Goodman and Flair married in 1971 and went on to have two children: David and Megan.

Flair and the Minnesota native were married for over a decade before divorcing in 1983.

Leslie has since remarried and now goes by the last name Jacobs and resides in Colorado.

Elizabeth Harrell

Flair's marriage to Harrell was the longest of all of them, lasting 23 years. It began in 1983.

Harrell and Flair had another two children together: Reid and Charlotte.

Harrell is an actress who has appeared in numerous reality TV shows and movies, including Celebrity Wife Swap.

Tiffany VanDemark

VanDemark and Flair were only together for three years.

Flair's marriage to the fitness instructor was tumultuous according to Grantland who reported, “In 2009, Flair filed a criminal complaint against Tiffany Vandemark — wife no. 3 — whom he accused of ‘hitting him in the face with a phone charger.'”

Financial troubles reportedly also caused a strain in their marriage, causing its quick end.

Jackie Beems

Jackie Beems' three-year marriage to Flair was tainted by assault allegations.

In 2010, the Associated Press reported, that Beems was “reportedly arrested…after she allegedly attacked the WWE Hall of Famer and left him bloodied and bruised.”

The charges were reportedly later dropped due to lack of evidence.

TMZ reported that court documents showed Beems alleged: “Husband has had numerous intimate relationships with other women during the parties’ marriage, to the embarrassment and humiliation of wife.”

There were even reports that Beems sent threatening messages to Flair's next wife, Wendy Barlow.

Who is Ric Flair's current wife?

On January 31, 2022, Flair took to Twitter to announce his split from Wendy Barlow.

Barlow, 61, is a Canadian All-American-ranked retired professional tennis player and coach.

While the two tied the knot in 2018, they actually met decades in 1993.

At the time, Barlow worked for Flair as the WCW valet "Fifi the French Maid."

After working together for years, the couple became romantically involved back in 2013 before getting engaged in 2016.

In 2013, Flair's son Reid died of an overdose at the age of 25.

Barlow shares five children of her own with a previous husband.
