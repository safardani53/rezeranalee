Mercy Miller dad, Master P, carved out his legacy well before the rise of many contemporary rappers in the music industry.

The Miller duo shares an exceptionally strong bond, with Master P consistently offering unwavering support to his son at every opportunity.

Born on September 13, 2005, Mercy Miller is the son of Master P and Soniya C. Miller.

Details Of Mercy Miller Dad Master P: Their Age Gap

Mercy is the youngest son of the rapper and producer Master P, whose real name is Percy Robert Miller Sr.

Percy was born on April 29, 1970, in the Third Ward of Uptown New Orleans, Louisiana.

He was the oldest sibling out of five. He attended Booker T. Washington High School and Warren Easton High School.

The rapper graduated with a degree in business administration. He opened his record store when he received $10k as an inheritance from his late grandfather.

In 1990, the rapper released his first cassette tape, “Mind of a Psychopath,” which gave a kick start to his musical career.

The same year, the death of his brother, Kevin Miller, increased his motivation to become a successful person to save his family.

As of 2023, Percy is 53 years old, and there is a 35-year age gap between Percy and Mercy who is 18 years old.

Likewise, Miller is an inch taller than his father, with Master 6’3″ and Mercy 6’4″.

Apart from Mercy, Percy has 8 other children with his ex-wife, Sonya C. Miller.

The Dynamic Miller Siblings

Romeo, born on August 19, 1989, is known as Lil Romeo. He is a rapper, actor, reality TV star, and record producer.

Additionally, their daughter Cymphonique was born in 1996. She is an actress and singer featured on the hit show How to Rock.
