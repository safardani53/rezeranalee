8 Best Wigs 2021 | The Sun UK

Publish date: 2024-08-31

THE best wigs don't even look like they're fake, giving you whatever colour or style tresses you fancy without having to touch your real hair.

Millions of people worldwide suffer from hair loss for medical reasons, which is where the best wigs can swoop in to serve some confidence, and everyone deserves to feel good.

If, like mega-influencer Kylie Jenner, you just fancy trying out a new hair colour almost everyday then you can opt for vibrant manes of reds, blues and yellows.

Just fancy something sleek? There are more natural, straight and curly ones which look just like the real thing. Looking to amp up your drag queen game? The bigger the better.

You'll find that most of your favourite stars often opt for wigs on the red carpet, which is why one day you might see them with bangs and the next they're gone.

When wearing a wig, you'll want to prep your hair first by flattening it and putting on a wig cap, then after your wig's in place we recommend applying a small amount of foundation to your hairline to blend better with your skin.

Whether you're feeling adventurous or just fancy switching up for your next glam party look, we've found the best wigs to shop now. Hair goes...

If you want Hollywood-worthy hair at normal-people prices, Lullabellz is the place to head.
