About Eric Decker

An American football wide receiver Eric Decker was born Eric Thomas Decker on March 15, 1987, in Cold Spring, Minnesota, United States.

Decker attended Rocori High School and was present when the Rocori High School shooting took place.

Eric saved himself by hiding in a cupboard along with other students until law enforcement rescued them.

After high school graduation, Eric attended the University of Minnesota.

While in university, he excelled in baseball and football but ultimately chose football to pursue as a professional career.

He was drafted in the third round of the 2010 NFL Draft by the Denver Broncos.

After playing with the Broncos for four seasons, he moved to New York Jets for three years and then to the Titans for one season.

Decker signed a one-year contract with the New England Patriots on August 2, 2018. However, 24 days later, he announced his retirement on August 26, 2018.
