Tim Hasselbeck Wife | Celiac Disease & Controversy

Elisabeth Hasselbeck was diagnosed with celiac disease during her sophomore year of college.

It is an autoimmune disease that damages the small intestine when a protein called gluten is digested.

Hasselbeck has published a book on the subject titled The G-Free Diet: A Gluten-Free Survival Guide which appeared on several best-seller lists.

However, on June 23, 2009, a lawsuit was filed against Hasselbeck in federal court in Massachusetts, asserting that her book was plagiarized from a book by Susan Hasset.

Hasselbeck denied the affirmation, claiming the allegations baseless. The suit was dismissed in November that year when the petitioner’s lawyer declined to pursue the case.
