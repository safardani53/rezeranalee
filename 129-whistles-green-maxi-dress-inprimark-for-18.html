Publish date: 2024-08-27

WHEN you fall in love with an outfit - but not the price tag - it’s hard to get it out of your head. 

But one keen shopper has revealed how she decided to shop around for a similar item to the dress she’d spotted - and save herself over £100 in the process. 

TikTok account house_on_the_crescent posted a video to her account to show how the original Whistles green maxi she had her heart set on compared to the bargain she’d picked up in Primark. 

Alongside footage of the floaty, tiered maxi on a model in the shop, she explained: “When you see a gorgeous dress in Whistles window for £129.”

The footage then cut to a very similar looking tiered dress with spaghetti straps hanging in her home. 

She continued: “But you find an almost identical one in Primark for £18.”

House_on_the_crescent then proudly showed off her bargain buy to her followers, as she twirled around to show the dress in all its glory.

She added in the caption: “When you find a bargain and have to share it.”

The fashion fan’s followers were also glad she’d chosen to upload it, as they said they would have snapped up the Primark number themselves. 

They flocked to the comment section to compliment the look - and the money-saving skills.
