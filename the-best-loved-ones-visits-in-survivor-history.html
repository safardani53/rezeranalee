The Best Loved Ones Visits In Survivor History

Publish date: 2024-02-27
Toward the end of some seasons of Survivor, the remaining castaways are treated to an overwhelming and heartfelt surprise—visits from their loved ones.

These special, and often tearful, reunions become some of the most talked-about moments of the season as fans watch their favorite players reconnect with parents, siblings, and spouses.

During Survivor: Winners at War, the players were treated to an unprecedented visit from loved ones. Jeremy was floored when not just one person, but his entire family came out to greet him.

In addition to this welcome surprise, every player this season was able to spend time with their families, not just the competition winners. A rare treat. 

Watch Jeremy reunite with his loved ones during Episode 10 of Survivor: Winners at War, "The Full Circle."
