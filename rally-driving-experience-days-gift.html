Best rally driving experience days 2022 | The Sun UK

Publish date: 2024-03-29

DRIVING a supercar around a track is all well and good, but the best rally experiences offer a whole different thrill. Whether on loose gravel, dirt or snow, rally driving demands skill and precision, testing the mettle of even the most seasoned racer.

If you fancy giving it a go, we’ve rounded up the best rally experiences around the UK.

Rally days can be a pretty complete test of your driving skill as the conditions are often a lot tougher than a spin on the tarmac.

So if you’re looking to give a great driving experience gift this year, consider snapping up a rally experience for maximum value for money.

How much are rally driving experience days?

A day at rally school can set you back a lot of money, costing up to around £300.

Don't worry though, we've done the hard work for you and found the best and most affordable rally experience days.

Depending on vehicle preference, driving time, and location, you could spend less than £100.

1. Virgin Experience Days

  • Introductory Rally Driving course, £179 - buy here

The top-rated experience from Virgin Experience Days is the Introductory Rally Driving course.

£179 gets you a lengthy training session with an instructor at eight locations in the UK where you get the chance to master the basics of rally driving.

Their day includes up to an hour on the track depending on location.

  • Double Rally Experience, £89 - buy here

If you want to try out different cars during your experience, here's something you might love.

With this Double Rally Experience you'll get two rally driving sessions and a certificate at the end of your day.

Available in two locations across the UK.

2. Buyagift

  • Extended Rally Driving Experience, £179 - buy here

Buyagift offers a number of top gifting ideas and experience days.

But the highest-rated rally day is an extended rally driving experience day at Brands Hatch in Kent.

The package costs £189 and buys you 40 minutes driving time in a works-prepared Toyota GT86.

That includes expert tuition from a qualified instructor and a high speed passenger lap to get your pulse racing.

3. Into The Blue

  • Rally Driving Yorkshire, from £95 - buy here

Into The Blue have got experience days with everything from flying, to water sports to spa days.

But their top-rated rally experience day is the Ford Escort Rally Driving day in Seaton Ross, Yorkshire.

There are two packages on offer through the website: either the taster session that gets you four laps and a trophy and bubbly for the fasted time, or a full day which includes more laps.

4. Experience Days

  • Supercar and rally driving experience, £99 - buy here

If you want the best of both worlds: supercars and rally cars, Experience Days have got you covered.

Their supercar and rally driving experience in Stafford gives drivers the chance to drive two high-performance sports cars before taking the wheel of a Subaru Impreza WRX STI.

£109 buys six laps in each car and some expert advice from a qualified instructor.

5. Trackdays

  • Extreme rage buggy experience, £49 - buy here

For something a little less conventional, you can throw a seriously powerful rage buggy around a rally track.

Trackdays has an experience in Oxfordshire, Leicestershire and Yorkshire that's received rave reviews and lets you drive a rage buggy that can go 0-60mph in under four seconds; that's faster than plenty of super cars, for the record.

The experience will set you back £49 and gets you 12 laps behind the wheel at breakneck speed.
