Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Wont Let Stormi Watch TV

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Travis Scott’s 10-month-old daughter Stormi will not be keeping up with the Kardashians. The rapper revealed that he and girlfriend Kylie Jenner aren’t allowing their little girl to watch television.

“Today’s kids are on iPads. There’s so much technology, they don’t play outside anymore,” Scott, 26, told Rolling Stone in an interview that was released on Thursday, December 20, noting that his Astroworld Festival in November took place outdoors. “With Stormi — no TV. That s—t is out.” 

Scott and the Kylie Cosmetics founder, 21, welcomed Stormi in February 2018. The Texas native believes they conceived Stormi three weeks after meeting.

“We felt like, ‘This is something special.’ And kids are something we kind of talked about when we were getting busy,” he told the magazine. The Grammy nominee had his fingers crossed for a boy. “At first I was like, ‘Man, I need a son,’” he recalled. “When we found out it was a girl, I was like, ‘Huhhh.’ But after a while I was like, ‘Yo, this might be the illest thing that ever happened.’ And when Stormi was born, I was like, ‘Life is fire, bro.’”

Last month, Scott revealed that Kylie’s mom, Kris Jenner, “held it down” in the delivery room. “She walked me through the whole process,” he explained on The Ellen DeGeneres Show. 

All of Scott’s first-time dad fears melted away as soon as Stormi was placed in his arms. “Going into it, I was nervous and scared, because we’re both young,” he said on the talk show. “But you know, when you first hold a baby in your arms, it’s uncontrollable. I never thought I could just love something so hard, ya know? It’s crazy.”
