Skyler Samuels Biography Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Bio:
Skyler Samuels, born Skyler Rose Samuels on April 14, 1994, is the daughter of Kathy Samuels, a producer for unscripted series, and Scott Samuels, a U.S. marshal. Skyler got her first acting break when her mother, who was a producer for Access Hollywood, sent her to cover a Harry Potter press junket when she was only 7 years old. Since then, she experienced a great deal of success as a child actress, with credits in several television shows and movies including, The Duff, The Gates and Furry Vengeance.

Best Known For:
Her starring roles in high profile television series such as Scream Queens with Lea Michelle and Emma Roberts and American Horror Story: Coven with Jessica Lange.

Personal Life:

She has three brothers, Cody, Harrison, and Jack, and one sister, Heather. She attended Stanford University.
