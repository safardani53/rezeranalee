'A big yes!' cheer fans as popular Dunkin' drink flavor is back on the menu and customers say 'this

Publish date: 2024-02-25

DUNKIN’ has released its new winter menu, with a host of returning items.

The launch made a splash on social media, where fans celebrated the release of a classic drink.

The post-Christmas launch includes a Pink Velvet Macchiato, as well as a handful of other new items.

The coffee and donuts chain, especially popular in the Northeast, rolled out the new items at stores across the country on December 27.

They’ll remain available for a limited time.

THE LAUNCH

The Pink Velvet Macchiato is a coffee drink with red velvet and cream cheese flavors.

Read More about Dunkin

Its distinctive color, brown over pink, makes the drink stand out.

The beverage is back for the first time in three years, drumming up significant anticipation.

It’s not the only returning item, however.

Dunkin’ has also brought back the Pancake Wake-up Wrap, which debuted last winter.

There are also two new items appearing at Dunkin’ locations nationwide.

The Frosty Red Velvet donut features vanilla ice cream with cream cheese-flavored sprinkles.

The brand is also introducing White Hazelnut Bark Coffee. The hazelnut and white chocolate flavored drink can be made hot or iced.

The rollout also marks the return of Sweet Black Pepper Seasoned Snackin’ Bacon.

CAFFEINE CRAVINGS

Dunkin’ fans have taken to X, formerly Twitter, to celebrate the returning menu items.

“The Iced Pink Velvet Macchiato from Dunkin is a big YES,” one user said.

Another had not got their hands on the Macchiato quite yet, but expressed excitement.

“DUNKIN IS BRINGING BACK THE PINK VELVET THIS IS NOT A DRILL,” they tweeted.
