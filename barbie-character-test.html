How to take the Barbie character test

Publish date: 2024-05-05

THE BARBIE character test enables fans to further connect with the various personalities from the film.

Following the July 21, 2023 release of the Barbie movie, fans want to know more about the test and how they can participate.

What is the Barbie character test?

The Barbie character test was created by IDR Labs.

It is an "unscientific and 'just for fun' test made popular by the popularity of the Barbie franchise."

It contains 35 questions that "determine which of seven Barbie characters you resemble the most."

People who take the test are assigned a specific Barbie personality based on their answers.

They can also see the percentage breakdown across the seven different characters.

Each of the personalities also contains a description that participants can see upon being assigned to that Barbie.

For example, Writer Barbie's description states, in part: "Writer Barbie is a talented and imaginative writer who uses her words to inspire and entertain.

"Writer Barbie often finds herself immersed in exciting adventures and quests as she brings her stories to life. With her love for literature, creativity, and expressive nature, she encourages her fans to embrace their own writing abilities and the power of storytelling."

Within the film's A-list cast, Margot Robbie stars as Barbie, while President Barbie is portrayed by Issa Rae.

Kate McKinnon plays Gymnast Barbie, Emerald Fennell has the role of Midge, and Dua Lipa plays Mermaid Barbie.

Writer Barbie is portrayed by Alexandra Shipp, while Emma Mackey plays Physicist Barbie.

How can I take the test?

You can take the test through IDR Labs' website.

Each question involves a statement that you choose to agree or disagree with via a sliding scale.

After completing the test, you have the option to share your results on social media, including Twitter and Facebook.
