Publish date: 2024-07-15

As the controversy around the suitress unfolds, The Bachelor Season 26 has become the Shanae Ankney show. Echard was astonished when the women told him of the occurrence, so he contacted Ankney to hear her side of the tale.

She said the truth, but she added that she wanted to apologize to the girls. Ankney made a teary-eyed apology, which she subsequently admitted on video was a forgery. She likened her acting to Meryl Streep’s and said that she is deserving of the Oscar, Emmy, and Golden Globe Awards.

Viewers criticize Shanae Ankney for her phony apologies
