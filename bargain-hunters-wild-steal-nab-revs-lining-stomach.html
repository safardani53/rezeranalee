Bargain hunters are going wild for a steal you can nab in Revs &amp; its perfect for lining your stom

Publish date: 2024-08-02

IF you are looking for a cheap meal out, you may wish to pop to Revs.

Bargain hunters have been sharing how the chain is offering £2.99 meals, so it could be a cheap way to line the stomach before a night out.

One person shared a photo of the sandwich board outside their local Revolutions.

They wrote on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK: “Might help a few people who fancy a meal out on a budget. 

“This is the Nottingham Revolution EDIT* but also available at all Revs.”

According to the Revs website: “Pick from five dishes and get change from a fiver!

More on food

“We're here to rescue your taste buds and put two fingers up to the cost of living crisis, one tasty bite at a time.”

From Sunday to Thursday, diners can choose from pizza, burgers and fries, a wrap, fish and chips or a fresh Asian salad for the deal.

And you can even add a soft drink for £1.50 or upgrade to a pint for £2.

All you need to do is book or walk into your local Revs and ask for the Meal Deal menu.

The website says: “For almost everyone it’s £2.99.

“If you’re in Leadenhall or Cambridge it’s £4.99.

“This offer is not available in York or Liverpool Albert Dock, but if you are in Liverpool, you can pop over our St. Peter's Square bar!!

Revs says the deal won’t be around forever, so “make the most of it while you can.”

They added: “Whether you after a speedy lunch out of the office, that essential weekday catch-up with the gang to spill the tea, or a solo date (rate it), our meal deal is here so you can do the things you love this summer, without breaking the bank.”

Many people seemed excited by the deal, with one saying: “omg that’s mint!”

Another added: “need to go!”
