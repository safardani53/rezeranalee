Jennifer Hudson Gets Fashion Advice from Son David Jr., 3

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Suri Cruise isn’t Hollywood’s only mini stylist.

Jennifer Hudson’s 3-year-old son David Jr. (with fiance David Otunga) is equally fashion-forward, the American Idol alum reveals, offering his mother totally unsolicited fashion advice.

“We call him Boss Baby!” Hudson tells Lucky mag‘s January issue (on stands December 11.) “He says, ‘Mommy, I don’t like that,’ or ‘Momma, you look very pretty.'”

Confesses the Oscar winner, “I totally dress for my child! I have T-shirts with Popeye the Sailor Man on it, ones with different animals, comic book characters . . . “

Hudson, 31, also credits her grandfather with shaping her style in a major way.

“The most stylish person in my family was definitely him,” she shares. “He’d take you into his closet and be like, ‘Hey! You need to wear this, Jenny!’ He was [never] dressed down, honey, his closet, his clothes, they were sharp. He would pick things out of his for me to wear and they were always perfect. That’s why I love menswear.”

Ever since her much-discussed 2010 slimdown, Hudson — who says she’s always adored fashion — has had even more fun with clothing, experimenting with different looks to see what’s most flattering on her new figure.

“It’s like I have more room to play,” the singer explains. “Sometimes I feel like I’m my own doll, dressing myself up. All of a sudden, a shirt can be a dress, you know?”

Her secret to keeping the weight off? Good old-fashioned exercise.

“Going jogging makes me feel powerful and free — like Rocky!” Hudson exclaims. “In winter I do the treadmill, the Stairmaster, I like challenging things. I always say to myself, ‘Just give me 10 minutes.’ If I do those 10 minutes — the next thing you know, it’s 20 minutes — I trick myself!”
