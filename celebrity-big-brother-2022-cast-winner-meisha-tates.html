Celebrity Big Brother: 2022 schedule and full cast revealed

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Celebrity Big Brother season 3 came to a wrap as Meisha Tates was crowned the winner with Todrick Hall as Runner up.

On February 2, 2022, 11 cast members walked through the Celebrity Big Brother doors, but there can only be one winner.

Who is in the Celebrity Big Brother cast?

Teddi Mellencamp - Evicted

Born July 1, 1981, Teddi Mellencamp is an American television personality, actress, and self-proclaimed accountability coach who starred in three seasons of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

Mellencamp also founded and owns All In by Teddi, a lifestyle and fitness company.

She is the daughter of musician John Mellencamp and is married to security company CEO Edwin Arroyave with whom she has three children.

Mellencamp is also the stepmother to Arroyave's daughter from a previous marriage.

Cynthia Bailey- Evicted

Cynthia Bailey is an actress, model, and reality TV personality from the US.

Bailey traveled to New York City at the age of 18 to pursue a modeling career after growing up in Alabama.

Bailey has been in films such as Without You I'm Nothing and For Love or Money and on television shows like The Cosby Show.

In 2010, Bailey became a cast member of the Bravo reality show The Real Housewives of Atlanta; however, she left the show officially in 2021.

Bailey married her first husband Peter Thomas in 2010, and the couple divorced in 2017. She is now married to sportscaster Mike Hill.

Todd Bridges - Evicted

Todd Bridges is an actor from the US, born on May 27, 1965, in California.

On the comedy Diff'rent Strokes, he played Willis Jackson, and on the sitcom Everybody Hates Chris, he played Monk.

Todrick Hall - Runner up

Born in Texas on April 4, 1985, Todrick Hall is an American singer, songwriter, choreographer, and YouTuber.

He rose to national prominence after reaching the semi-finals of the ninth season of the television singing competition American Idol.

Following that, he built a YouTube following with viral videos that included original music, parodies, and sketches.

He strives to be a role model for LGBTQ+ individuals and people of color, and his art incorporates his own experiences as a black homosexual man.

Chris Kattan - Quit

Christopher Kattan is an American actor, comedian, and novelist who was born on October 19, 1970.

He is most known for his appearances on Saturday Night Live, as Doug Butabi in A Night at the Roxbury, as Bob on The Middle for the first four seasons, and Bunnicula in Bunnicula.

Chris Kirkpatrick - Evicted

Christopher Kirkpatrick is an American singer, dancer, composer, music producer, and sometimes actor.

He is best known as a founding member of the musical group NSYNC, where he performed countertenor.

He has voiced Chip Skylark on The Fairly OddParents and has featured on various TV series.

He also appeared as himself on The Simpsons in the episode "New Kids on the Blecch," alongside his fellow NSYNC bandmates.

Carson Kressley - Evicted

Carson Kressley is an American television personality, actor, and designer who was born on November 11, 1969.

He rose to notoriety after appearing on Bravo's Queer Eye for the Straight Guy in 2003.

Kressley was also a competitor on season 13 of Dancing with the Stars and the motivating presenter of the TV program How to Look Good Naked and OWN's Carson Nation.

He has been a judge on RuPaul's Drag Race since 2015 and he paired up with his former Queer Eye alum Thom Filicia for a new Bravo series called Get a Room, which premiered in October 2018.

Shanna Moakler - Evicted

Shanna Moakler is a model, actress, and reality television personality from the US.

She was the first runner-up in Miss USA 1995 and the winner of the Miss New York USA pageant in 1995.

Moakler took over as Miss USA when Chelsi Smith won the Miss Universe contest in 1995.

She began modeling at the age of 15 and was named Playmate of the Month in December 2001 by Playboy magazine.

Moakler was married to Travis Barker.

Mirai Nagasu - Evicted

Mirai Aileen Nagasu is a Japanese-American figure skater.

She is a three-time Four Continents medalist, having won silver in 2016, bronze in 2011 and 2017.

She was also a 2007 JGP Final winner, two-time World Junior medalist, winning silver in 2007, bronze in 2008.

Nagasu is also a seven-time US national medallist, winning gold in 2008, silver in 2010 and 2018, bronze in 2011 and 2014, pewter in 2016 and 2017.

Lamar Odom- Evicted

Lamar Odom is a retired professional basketball player from the United States.

He won titles with the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA) in 2009 and 2010 and was voted NBA Sixth Man of the Year in 2011.

From 2009 to 2016, Odom was married to reality star KhloÃ© Kardashian.

Odom made multiple appearances on the reality television show Keeping Up with the Kardashians throughout their marriage.

Miesha Tate - Winner

Miesha Tate is a mixed martial artist from the United States.

She is a former Strikeforce Women's Bantamweight Champion and a former UFC Women's Bantamweight Champion.

Tate served as the Vice President of ONE Championship.

She is currently ranked no. 8 in the UFC women's bantamweight rankings as of December 20, 2021.
