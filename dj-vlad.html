Who is DJ Vlad:

DJ Vlad is a famous DJ. He was born on June 28, 1973 and his birthplace is Ukraine. DJ is also well known as, DJ and businessman most famous for being the founder and CEO of the hip-hop news and gossip website VladTV. He is also the director of the BET documentary series American Gangster and co-created the critically-acclaimed mixtape Rap Phenomenon.

DJ is originated from Ukraine. He was awarded $300,000 in a civil suit after being assaulted by the entourage of rapper Rick Ross in 2008.
