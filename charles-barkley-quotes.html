Charles Barkley is a famous name in the history of Basketball. He is a former American professional basketball player and politician. He has several nicknames according to his different behavior. He is called “Sir Charles” due to his aggressive and outspoken demeanor, and for his unique build and talent as a player, he is named “The Round Mound of Rebound.” He is also called a Chunk Barkley by most of his friends.

Moreover, He is an influential person as he set himself as one of the National Basketball Association’s dominating power forwards. For the victory, he had a versatile violent game. His popularity increases enormously due to his talent, and now he is regarded as one of the best rebounders in the game’s history. Despite his skill and dedication, he was not able to win an NBA championship during his career. He is not known only as a player; he is an analyst on the TV show known as Inside the NBA.
