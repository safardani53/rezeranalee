What is Neil Young's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Neil Young has an estimated net worth of around $200million.

He is known for his expansive music catalogue, featuring multiple hit songs including Heart of Gold and Harvest Moon.

In 2010, Young won a Grammy for Best Boxed or Special Edition Limited Package, for his The Archives Vol. 1 1963–1972 tapes, as well as the Person of the Year award.

The following year, he won a Grammy for Best Rock Song with Angry World.

Young is also a two-time member of The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

Throughout his career, he's released over 30 albums and has directed a multitude of films.

How old is Neil Young?

Born on November 12, 1945 in Toronto, Neil Young is currently 76 years old.

After moving to Ontario as a child, Young contracted polio and became partially paralyzed on the left side of his body.

Inspired by the likes of Elvis Presley, Chuck Barry, and Little Richard, Young taught himself the ukulele.

He went on to join the band The Squires, playing shows around Canada for years.

Young eventually left the band and took on his home country as a solo artist, before moving to Los Angeles and joining Buffalo Springfield.
