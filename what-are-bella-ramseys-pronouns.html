BELLA Ramsey has become a fan favorite after the star's role as Lyanna Mormont on Game of Thrones.

Ramsey recently landed another role as Ellie in HBO's newest series The Last of Us.

Who is Bella Ramsey?

Isabella May Ramsey was born on September 30, 2003, in Nottingham, United Kingdom.

Though having been on other projects, the English star had built quite the name for themselves when they took on the role of Lyanna Mormont on Game of Thrones.

More recently, the 19-year-old was cast as Ellie on The Last of Us alongside former GoT alumni, Pedro Pascal.

The Last of Us duo quickly became a fan favorite due to their chemistry on and off the big screen as well as their individual personalities.

What are Bella Ramsey's pronouns?

In an interview with The New York Times, Ramsey admits that she is gender fluid.

Ramsey said: "I guess my gender has always been very fluid. Someone would call me 'she' or 'her' and I wouldn't think about it, but I knew that if someone called me 'he' it was a bit exciting.

"I'm very much just a person. Being gendered isn't something that I particularly like, but in terms of pronouns, I really couldn't care less."
