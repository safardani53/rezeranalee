John Mayer Finds Gay Clubs the Most Respectful

Publish date: 2024-08-02

The internet is going wild! Is singer John Mayer gay or straight? Oh my, has he switched “teams” as some websites are calling it. These rumors started when he was seen at gay club Toucan’s Tiki Lounge last weekend.

Some bloggers were suggesting that he fled the club after being kissed on the lips by a male party-goer. Didn’t happen that way according to Mayer. John enjoyed his night out tremendously and took to his Twitter page to set the record straight.

“Rumor check: Went to a gay bar in Palm Springs, yes. Had a blast. Danced my face off. Someone there planted a kiss on me? No. I don’t like the story painting gays as unable to control themselves. Found the crowd even more respectful than in a straight club. You know who the flamboyant crowd is? Straight, drunk girls. They’re like a bunch of little Charles Nelson Reilly’s.”

We have to agree with John’s observation about straight drunk girls….they are crazy! This isn’t the first time that Mayer has had to put the kibosh on the kissing men rumors. Back in 2007 celebrity gossip blogger Perez Hilton started his own rumor, saying that he kissed John at a New Years Eve party. That was vehemently denied by Mayer.
