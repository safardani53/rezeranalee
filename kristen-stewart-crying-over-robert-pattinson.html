Kristen Stewart: Crying Over Robert Pattinson?

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Kristen Stewart and Robert Pattinson appeared to be having a Thanksgiving weekend together … until she was spotted leaving her dad’s house in tears.

The actress’ day with her family came to a crashing halt as she bolted her father’s house on November 29. Photographers saw her crying as she departed.

Was she brought to tears because Rob wasn’t there?

Camera flashes went off as K-Stew made her way to her car, and tears streamed down her face from the swarm of attention, according to X17 Online.

Some of Kristen’s friends and family members appeared to be trying to console her, but it was no use as the star looked extremely shaken up.

After pushing away people and rebuffing attempts to help her and dissuade her from leaving, finally, she got into her vehicle alone and drove off.

 

Her holiday had been going so smoothly, too.

On Thanksgiving Day, Stewart was seen spotted looking happy as she and a friend walked to a grocery store to pick up some last-minute ingredients.

The next day, Kristen was then spotted walking near her dad’s house, and appeared content. A source said “she seemed happy and looked relaxed.”

While it’s easy to point finger at Rob’s absence as the source of Kristen’s anguish, because that makes for good celebrity gossip, it’s all very unclear.

Remember, this report comes on the heels of others saying that Kristen Stewart and Robert Pattinson are 100 percent back together and serious.

The fact that Rob was nowhere in sight could’ve been the reason she was upset, sure … but more likely it means he had nothing to do with it at all.
