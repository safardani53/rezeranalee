Who is Danny Aarons from Twitch?

Publish date: 2024-09-28

LIVE streaming is a massive industry, and some of the most successful streamers have managed to make a BIG name for themselves.

Here is everything we know about the popular gaming content creator, Danny Aarons.

Who is Danny Aarons?

Danny Aarons is a 21-year-old British Gaming YouTuber with over 1.49 million subscribers.

He started developing an interest in football and gaming at a young age.

He now produces FIFA-related content, as well as football content more broadly.

He has been posting FIFA content to his YouTube channel since 2018 and has amassed over 580 million views in total.

Aarons is not just popular on YouTube, but also has a huge following on TikTok, Instagram, and the live streaming site, Twitch.

He posts videos of himself playing FIFA and trying different things on the game.

One of his most popular videos, with 1.2 million views, is titled, "I spent $1000 on FIFA 22 accounts".

What is Danny Aarons' height?

According to several internet sources, including InfoFamousPeople.com, Aarons is 5ft 8 inches tall.

He was born in London in May 2002.

Which games does Danny Aarons stream on Twitch?

Aarons predominantly streams himself playing FIFA on his Twitch channel.

Past videos include him live-streaming himself playing the new FIFA 23 which received over 260,000 views.

FIFA releases a new, updated version of their game every year and the next FIFA 24 is out on September 22, 2023.

As a gaming content creator, however, Aarons gets to try out the game before the rest of the world.

He also did a giveaway for the game, allowing his followers the chance to have it before anyone else.

The gaming YouTuber has a schedule on his Twitch page that lets his followers know what he will be live-streaming and when.

Aarons' current Twitch schedule is rigorous, with him posting five times a week and streaming for at least three hours at a time.

Aarons has 733,000 followers on Twitch and often receives over 20,000 views on his live streams.
