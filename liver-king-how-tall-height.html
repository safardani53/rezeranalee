How tall is Liver King?

Publish date: 2024-06-15

INFLUENCER Liver King shot to fame when he introduced the world to his love of all things offal.

The fitness fanatic is always showing off his impressive muscles on TikTok, with some fans wondering just how tall he is.

How tall is Liver King?

Liver King - who's real name is Brian Johnson - stands at 5ft 7ins tall, and weighs around 190lbs.

He maintains his physique by following a diet of raw beef liver, protein shakes, egg yolks and bone marrow.

Speaking to GQ, he said he eats around a pound of raw liver every day.

However he recommends others start with three ounces, two times a week.

How old is Liver King?

Liver King was born on April 7, 1977 in Texas, which makes him 45-years-old.

In his viral videos on TikTok and Instagram, the bodybuilder can be seen submerging himself in ice baths, and catching fish straight from the sea with his teeth, in a bid maintain a healthy lifestyle.

He lives a relatively minimalist lifestyle, sleeping on a bed made from wooden pallets.

Liver King claims that he doesn't allow any mobile phones inside his bedroom, and has installed Faraday curtains on the windows to prevent radiowaves passing through.

What is Liver King's net worth?

Liver King is estimated to have a fortune of around $310million, according to The 24/7 Project.

He also owns a 8,300 sq ft mansion in Austin, Texas, where he lives with his wife Barbara AKA Liver Queen, and their two kids.

Liver King has amassed his wealth in a short space of time.

He posted his first ever TikTok video in August 2021, and since then has built up a huge following on social media and is one of the most popular influencers on the app.

As of October 2022, on TikTok he has 3.3M followers, and on Instagram he has 1.7M - both help to generate money for him.
