Who is Hillary Scott's husband Chris Tyrrell?

Publish date: 2024-03-11

HILLARY Scott has become a household name as part of country music group Lady A.

During her time in the band, she met her future husband Chris Tyrell – whom she claims was an inspiration for some of her music.

Who is Hillary Scott's husband Chris Tyrrell?

Hillary's husband Chris is a drummer and a full-time member of Lady A (formerly known as Lady Antebellum).

The pair met during their time at college but they didn't date until reconnecting while both serving as opening acts on Tim McGraw's Southern Voice tour in 2010.

At the time, Chris was the drummer for band Love And Theft.

When Lady Antebellum released Just a Kiss in May 2011, Scott hinted that the song was inspired by her relationship with Tyrrell.

Scott said: "We love writing songs from personal experience, and this is definitely one of those songs.

"There is so much excitement at the beginning of a new relationship ... all the butterflies and that optimistic feeling that this person could be 'the one.'

When did Hillary Scott get married?

Tyrrell proposed to Scott on July 2, 2011, during a family vacation in Tennessee.

The pair later tied the knot at a private ceremony in New York on January, 7, 2012.

How many children does Hillary Scott have?

Hillary and husband Chris are the proud parents of three daughters.

Their eldest daughter Eisele Kaye was born in 2013, followed by twins Betsy Mack and Emory JoAnn in 2018.

The family reportedly reside in a 7,600-square-foot (710 m2), six bedroom and eight bathroom home in Brentwood, Tennessee.
