Squid Game The Challenge love triangle rumors debunked as player talks shower situatio

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Squid Game: The Challenge faces ‘love triangle’ rumors after Phalisia claimed there were people hooking up during filming. She appeared on The Viall Files podcast stating she was in the bathroom while both players involved were in the shower. Showmances were definitely underway, she says.

Phalisia kept her lips sealed on the player’s names who allegedly hooked up on Squid Game: The Challenge. However, she urged listeners to search for the Netflix show’s love triangle “on the Internet”. It’s not just Phalisia spilling serious tea – but also Ashley, player 278, who revealed which stars are ‘involved’.

Squid Game The Challenge ‘love triangle’

Rumors of a “love triangle” was formed while filming Squid Game: The Challenge are swirling. Although none of the romances were aired on Netflix, Phalisia revealed on The Viall Files there were hook-ups.

She then claimed condoms were available to the players. Sam Kohn also told The Tab: “They had condom dispensers in the bathrooms and everyone was rubbing the lube from the condoms on their lips.”

She’s not the first to admit this, as another player, Stephen, claimed contestants were kicked off for hooking up in the hotels where contestants isolated before filming even began.

Phalisia talks juicy shower situation

Phalisia reveals there was a “stock of condoms” available to contestants. She said, without naming names: “I can say that I was in the bathroom when they both were in the shower.”

Sam had also said there were “modesty showers for the Muslim players which was floor to ceiling frosted glass” and there were “rumours people were f***ing in them”. He added: “I don’t know how true that is!”

However, Phalisia said she “doesn’t know” if the two people who hooked up slept together.

Netflix has been contacted for comment GRV Media and Reality Titbit.

Ashley claims two players were involved

Ashley alleged Joy, player 271, and Alex, 417, were involved in a love triangle. She was responding to a reveal by fellow player Dash Katz that Joy and Alex were in a showmance.

He said they were both eliminated in the cookie challenge. Squid Game player 188, Sam Kohn, backed up the claims as he told The Tab: “There was a little showman between players Joy and Alex.”

“He was on the bunk above me and they’d lie in bed each night and cuddle a bit, but then when it was lights out you had to go back to your own bunk,” Kohn added. Joy said she “misses” Alex.
