THE BIG D has made waves on social media after its explosive series premiere on the USA network.

The Big D is a new show on USA, and the first season premiered on Wednesday night.

The premise of the show features multiple recently divorced couples who are all looking for love.

The setup is similar to Bachelor in Paradise because no one is off-limits.

All of the couples are dating for the first time since their divorce, and most have only been divorced for a few months.

The show will uncover whether or not the contestants can fall in love with someone new, or if they'll end up with their ex again.

Most couples have at least one person who is on the show in an attempt to get their ex back, and it's already caused some drama.

Each week, the group will go through exercises meant to encourage them to find love, and they will be able to go on dates.

Each week, the group will vote out one person who doesn't seem like they're ready to be in a new relationship.

The goal of the show is to find one couple who is "ready to enter the real world in a committed relationship," according to the hosts JoJo Fletcher and Jordan Rodgers from The Bachelorette.
