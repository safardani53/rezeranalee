Madonna's Ex-Boyfriend Poses in Bathing Suit

Publish date: 2024-02-29

The Material Girl is missing out.

Jesus Luz, Madonna‘s ex-boyfriend, shows off his beach bod in a red-hot, briefs-style bathing suit for Page Six Magazine – despite dipping temperatures.

He may be back on the market, but the Brazilian model, 24, says he doesn’t plan on keeping his bachelor status forever.

“I want to be married and have children at some point in my life,” he says.

But for now, he’s prowling the dating scene in Manhattan, where he moved to six years ago to learn English. His means of happiness are simple: “food, friends, music and good sex.”

“There are many beautiful women in New York City,” he says. “It’s cool because there is such a mixture of ethnicities and cultures.”
