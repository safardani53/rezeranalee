McDonald's fans have learned about a "secret" menu that's only available in one state.

One McDonald's restaurant has international items, so fans can now get global food items without traveling across the world.

The unique menu can be found in McDonald's global headquarters in Chicago, Illinois.

The $250million building opened in 2018, and it was an initiative to create a more modern and progressive burger organization.

Within the headquarters, there is a restaurant open to the public where you can find international menu items.

An Instagram influencer, who goes by sarahmargareteats, visited the headquarters and found fries and burgers that aren't on the typical menu.

EXOTIC FOOD ITEMS

"This McDonald's literally has sweet potato fries, pistachio McFlurries, McPops, and more," Sarah said.

The restaurant currently has international items from Korea, Netherlands, and more.

From Korea, the chain has the Triple Onion Burger for $6.99.

The Pistachio McFlurry is from Italy, and it costs $3.19.

McDonald's also has different flavors of McPops, which are filled donut holes from Spain.

They have flavors like chocolate hazelnut, which can be bought in a pack of four for $3.99.

McDonald's regular fries are world-famous already, but they have Sweet Potato Fries for just $3.49 from the Netherlands.

The international menu items are not always available as the fast food chain rotates the food every few months

HAMBURGER UNIVERSITY

Besides the restaurant in the McDonald's headquarters, there is also a school within the building.

Hamburger University was founded by McDonald’s senior chairman Fred Turner in 1961.

More than 80,000 restaurant managers, mid-managers, and owner-operators have graduated since then.

If you want to go to Hamburger University, you might be able to but you must demonstrate exemplary work performance and managerial traits.

There are currently eight campuses around the world, located in Chicago, Tokyo, London, Sydney, Munich, São Paulo, Shanghai, and Moscow.

MORE LIMITED EDITION ITEMS

If you're not in the Chicago area to get the Pistachio McFlurry, you may be able to get a different flavor that's only available for a limited time.

A popular dessert that usually comes back on St. Patrick's Day is coming back early.

The fast food chain has announced the return of both the Shamrock Shake and its Oreo Shamrock McFlurry counterpart.

Both treats will be available at participating locations on February 20, which is almost a month before St. Patrick's Day.
