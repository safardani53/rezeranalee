The View's Whoopi Goldberg blasts 'stay out of my uterus' in angry rant on live TV

Publish date: 2024-07-10

THE View host Whoopi Goldberg has blasted: "Stay out of my uterus" in an angry rant while on live TV.

The most recent installment of The View dived into a heated discussion about the Supreme Court overturning Roe v. Wade. 

Guest hosts Chelsea Clinton, 42, and Alyssa Farrah Griffin, 33, shared their thoughts on the ramifications of the landmark decision, along with Sunny Hostin, 43.

Chelsea mentioned the "harassment" and "demonization of healthcare providers."

Alyssa added: "This is what drives me crazy about the current Republican party.  

"It's not in step with what conservative values are."

Whoppi jumped in when Alyssa spoke about the ban on interstate travel.

The Ghost actress ranted: "I always thought that conservatism always thought: "We have to do this, but we don't want you in our business.

"So okay! Get out of my uterus!

"Get me to my doctor! Regardless of what's happening in my body, you don't know. You don't know who's going to get an abortion."

She continued: "We're not telling you! You don't know why people are leaving your state! So don't pretend you know!

Whoopi concluded: "Because what you've done is make a problem when there wasn't one!"

This is not the first time the View host got heated during a topical issue.

GUN CONTROL

Recently, the ABC show kickstarted with the hosts discussing gun control.

During the debate with Ana Navarro, 50, and Sunny, the 66-year-old hostess asked what could President Joe Biden do at this time "when we haven't had anything done."

The Ghost actress: "The question remains, what can he do? What's missing here?"

She continued: "Should this power be in the hands of the president?"

When Ana was about to make her point and replied, "I think he's doing what he can...," that was when Whoopi interrupted and burped loudly.

Ana asked: "You okay?"

Whoopi excused herself and responded: "I was talking and burping at the same time."

The audience laughed as the Sister Act star poked fun at herself during the on-air blunder.

Ana then went back to the discussion on gun control. 

MISSING JOY

Meanwhile, the ladies of The View returned to their set in New York City after previously taking a trip to the Bahamas, where they celebrated 25 seasons of the daytime talk show.

Since their return, Joy Behar, 79, has recently been absent from the talk show.
