François "Frans" Botha is a former professional South African boxer and kickboxer. In boxing he competed from 1990 to 2014 and is perhaps best known for winning the IBF heavy belt against Axel Schulz in 1995, but was later disqualified after failing to test. He then challenged the world heavyweight titles twice in 2000 and 2002. Botha played against former heavyweight champions Mike Tyson, Wladimir Klitschko, Lennox Lewis, Michael Moorer and at the end of their careers, Evander Holyfield. Botha once participated in mixed martial arts in 2004 .
