Friends Fest is back - here are the dates for London, Manchester, Bristol and Milton Keynes and how

Publish date: 2024-08-19

FRIENDS Fest is making its return this summer, almost 25 years since the first episode ever aired.

But how do you get tickets? We've got all the info here.

What is Friends Fest?

It's self-proclaimed to be the "ultimate celebration of the world's favourite TV show" - which pretty much sums it up.

Based in four locations across Britain - London, Bristol, Manchester and Milton Keynes - the event gives hardcore Friends fans the opportunity to live as one of the gang for a day.

It allows you to walk around the most iconic sets, like Monica's apartment and the Central Perk coffee shop, and get some great snaps in the process.

Whether it be lounging around on the Central Perk couch, or recreating the Thanksgiving floating heads scene by peeping around Monica's apartment door, there's plenty of opportunities for fans to get involved.

There's even a chance for you to yell 'PIVOT' in the stairwell with a giant mattress... should you wish to recreate the iconic exchange between Rachel, Ross and Chandler.

Naturally, guests will be given the opportunity to recreate the title scenes' most notable moments, complete with umbrellas, a fake fountain and a whole lot of dancing.

And if that's not enough to convince you to go - you can even drink at a Friends-themed bar while taking part in a quiz on the show.

When is it returning to the UK?

The fan-favourite festival will be returning on August 2, kicking off its run in Manchester.

Yes, that's right you can be sipping coffee at the iconic Central Perk coffeehouse between August and September.

Londoners will get the chance to enjoy the FriendsFest between September 20-29 in Kennington Park.

But the good news is it will also be popping up in Milton Keynes and Bristol too.

Friends Fest 2019

The tour dates for this year are:

  • Manchester - August 2 to August 18
  • Bristol - August 23 to September 1
  • Milton Keynes - September 6 to September 15
  • London - September 20 to September 29

What is Friends Festive?

If you missed out on tickets to Friend Fest 2019, don't despair yet.

Eager fans still have the chance to nab a ticket to Friends Festive - a winter extravaganza which promises to be just as good as its summery counterpart.

Fancy wrapping up in your winter warmers to visit your favourite set during the festive season? Then this is the event for you...

From ringing Phoebe's bell outside the mall to re-enacting THAT iconic Thanksgiving floating heads scene, it's sure to be an unmissable event.

There will even be the chance to recreate the titles in the snow, and pay a visit to the holiday Armadillo's grotto.

The event will take place from November 28 to December 21 2019 at The Truman Brewery, Shoreditch, London.

The festival site is open everyday from 11am until 10pm.

Of course, there will also be the chance to get your hands on exclusive merchandise, as well as festive food and drinks.

How can I get tickets?

Unfortunately, tickets for Friends Fest 2019 are now sold out across all venues.

Tickets were £29 and went on sale at 10am Friday March 15.

You can get your hands on tickets for Friends Festive here.

They are £32 plus a £2.50 booking fee.
