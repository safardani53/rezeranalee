Zonnique Pullins Carries Gun At The Airport Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-13

Zonnique, who is also identified as a past member of the OMG Girlz R&B group was in possession of a Ruger 380 semi-automatic pistol, according to the police report obtained by E! News. Upon spotting the weapon on the X-Ray Machine, a supervisor was immediately notified of the potential danger. The blonde beauty, who also is featured on T.I. & Tiny: The Family Hustle, told authorities that she did have a weapons permit, although she was lacking the paperwork to prove her story. Police said that she was placed under arrest and whisked away, in addition to having her gun confiscated. Zonnique was transferred to Clayton Country Jail before being released on June 17.

T.I. Concert Shooting — PICS

Zonnique faces a misdemeanor charge for carrying a weapon at a commercial service airport without a permit. She has yet to make a statement about the incident via social media. T.I. and Tiny also have yet to comment either, but this news likely comes as unsettling to them. The pair, who married in July 2010, have three children together, their two boys Clifford ‘King’ Joseph Harris III, 12, and Major Philant Harris, 8. They also welcomed their daughter Heiress Diana Harris to the world on March 26. T.I. has three other children from previous relationships, daughter Deyjah Imani Harris, 15, and two older sons, Domani Uriah Harris, 15, and Messiah Ya’Majesty Harris, 14.

Zonnique’s arrest involving a gun may be especially heart-wrenching for T.I. in particular, due to the tragic shooting that occurred at his Irving Park concert in New York on May 25. One man was murdered and three others were also hurt as the audience fled for their lives before the rapper hit the stage. T.I. posted to his Instagram, “My heart is heavy today. Our music is intended to save lives, like mine and many others. My heartfelt condolences to the family that suffered the loss and my prayers are with those injured. Respectfully, Tip.”
