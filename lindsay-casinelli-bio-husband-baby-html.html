Lindsay Casinelli’s Work Experience Worth More Than Her Net Worth

Lindsay began her career in Los Angeles, where she went on to study journalism. In 2009, she took her first professional step as a news reporter in New York City. Moreover, she also hosted the magazine show of NBA team, El Show de Los Lakers.
