Liz Hurley wears plunging black dress as she continues idyllic New Year trip to India with her 16-ye

Publish date: 2024-06-22

LIZ Hurley made sure to command attention in a plunging black dress during her idyllic trip to India today.

The 53-year-old actress has been enjoying a New Year's getaway with her 16-year-old son Damian and rang in 2019 in Bombay.

The Royals star took to Instagram to post a sultry snap in a daring black crochet look, which highlighted her cleavage.

The racy garment also featured cutout detailing around her midriff and accessorised the ensemble with a pair of statement drop-earrings.

She worked her hair into a tousled curl and sported a dramatic smokey eye with a nude lip.

Captioning the photo, she wrote: "More #BombayNights #India" with a love heart emoji.

Elizabeth's sultry photo left her fans commenting on her age-defying appearance.

One follower wrote: "Don't know about Bombay nights. But this cold morning in London just got hotter."

"Better looking than any woman half your age," a second posted. "Are you sure you’re 53?"

Another shared: "Are you immortal? You look younger than me and you were one of my first celebrity crushes in Bedazzled when I was 11".

Her sultry display comes after she shared a number of photos with her teenage son Damian on New Year's Eve in India.

The Bedazzled actress rung in the New Year in style in a plunging purple gown while her teenage son wore traditional Indian dress for the special occasion.

Sharing snippets of the glamorous evening with her 1.1 million Instagram followers, the British model paired her suitably shimmery dress with a dramatic smokey eye and diamond chandelier earrings.

Finishing off her NYE look with a glossy blow dry, Liz completed her statement ensemble with a pair of silver metallic heels.

Meanwhile, Liz's son Damian wore traditional Indian dress for the evening in Bombay along with an embroidered white tunic.

The aspiring actor and model - who is the son of American businessman Steve Bing - also opted to go barefoot.
