Louise Redknapp shares Naked throwback video after ex-husband Jamie 'moves on' with French model

Publish date: 2024-05-21

LOUISE Redknapp took fans on a trip down memory lane when she shared a throwback video from her track Naked.

The 43-year-old revealed that she was learning a new routine for her hit solo track with none other than Madonna's choreographer Jamie King.

Louise looked incredible as she showed off her fancy moves in the throwback video of her solo track Naked.

The track was the third song she released from her debut album Naked in 1996, and reached number 5 in the charts.

She looked fantastic in an all white outfit - a fitted shirt and trousers that showed off her tiny frame.

In the video Louise wore a  shirt that was cropped to reveal her flat tummy and her slim waist.

Louise Redknapp gives extraordinary insight into life as a single mum as she admits she's 'not brushed her hair and has no time to do food shopping'

Her hair was in an on-trend feathered bob and fans could see a peak of her black bra underneath her white laced up top.

She wrote on Instagram alongside the video: "It’s been a busy couple of weeks rehearsing for my summer shows... and relearning this routine for Naked which I did with Madonna’s choreographer the legendary Jamie King."

Her fans loved it, with one writing: "I learnt this choreo when I was a young dancer and loved it' my favourite video by far!" While another said: "Rememberrrrr we loved this."

It came as her ex husband Sky pundit Jamie, 44, "moved on" with French model Julia Restoin Roitfeld.

Julia, 37, was born in Paris and is a model and art director - who is currently based in New York.

She also works as graphic designer for clients such as Max Mara, Miu Miu and Altuzzara and has a five-year-old daughter to her ex-boyfriend, Swedish model Robert Konjic.
