Jennifer Lopez Strips Down to Underwear in Movie Trailer

Publish date: 2024-07-01

Jennifer Lopez is bringing the thrills to the action film Parker. 

The 43-year-old actress strips down to her underwear in the new trailer in front of costar Jason Statham, 45.

“Take off your clothes,” Statham, who plays professional thief Parker, tells Lopez. “I have to know if you’re wearing a wire.”

Lopez obeys and flaunts her sexy, toned bod in matching polka-dot bra and underwear in the process.

Parker is on a mission to hunt down his former team who doubled crossed him and took all his money. He enlists the help of Leslie (Lopez), a good-looking insider who’s looking for cash.

But a little romance occurs along the way. The trailer shows a brief flash of a steamy shower kiss between the two stars!
