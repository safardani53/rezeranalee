Publish date: 2024-04-19

A BIG busted young woman has decided to design a supportive bikini for other ladies that share her proportions.

Vanessa Sanchez (@nessyswimwear) knows how it feels to squeeze 'the girls' into a bikini that just doesn't fit quite right.

Now, the confident entrepreneur is building a diverse swimwear line that is made for ladies who have large breasts.

In a recent video, she modeled one of her favorite pieces, the Double String Halter Top in Turquoise Shimmer.

"Made a bikini for my big-chested girlies," the bold brunette wrote in the caption.

Sanchez showed how the bright blue bikini offers not one, but two straps for added support.

She added the text "instant lift" over the video, indicating that the double straps help perk up the 'tatas.'

On her website, Sanchez explained her vision behind the company, Nessy Swimwear.

"Growing up, I faced many struggles with my body," the young woman wrote. "As a Mexican-American woman, I was blessed with many curves..."

Sanchez admitted that it took her some time to accept her body and embrace her shape.

Now at age 20, she has spent the last six years developing bikinis that are made to "bring women confidence."

Sanchez's collection features an array of styles, colors, and sizes. Most of the items retail for under $50.

Many of her followers have expressed their gratitude for the all-inclusive swimwear line.

"You are my savior. Looking at website now, I can never find cute supportive bathing suits," one person commented on her video.

Other people said that they adored the style, but were concerned about the weight of the straps.

"Love this but the around the neck part always gives me headaches after wearing for too long," the TikToker commented.

Sanchez responded that she also has designed bikinis that are completely adjustable for ultimate comfort.

With summer just around the corner, now is a great time for Sanchez to spread the word about her fashion line.
