Cristiano Ronaldo posts naked snap in bath during 'recovery time' as Juventus star relaxes after Cop

Publish date: 2024-08-29

CRISTIANO RONALDO shared a picture on Instagram in his bath as he recovered from Juventus’ Coppa Italia loss against Atlanta.

The Juve star posted a picture on his Instagram enjoying a bath as he captioned the post: “Recovery time. Good night everyone.”

Ronaldo played the full 90 minutes as his side were dumped out of the Coppa Italia in the quarter-finals.

Atlanta were 2-0 at half-time before Duvan Zapata killed the game off as Juve sunk three goals.

Juve have won the trophy four years on the trot, with Napoli the last side to win the Coppa Italia back in 2014.

Despite crashing out of the cup, Massimo Algeria's side still top Serie A by 11 points with Napoli chasing in second.

Ronaldo is the second highest goalscorer in the league netting 15 times, behind only Sampdoria’s Fabio Quagliarella who has one more.
