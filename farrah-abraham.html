Farrah Abraham News, Photos And Videos Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Bio:
Farrah Abraham (born Farrah Abraham on May 31, 1991 in Omaha, Nebraska) is a reality star, author, and aspiring musician and model. She was featured on the MTV series 16 & Pregnant and Teen Mom for four seasons. She has also appeared on Celebrity Big Brother and Couple’s Therapy, the only person to ever do so without a partner. Farrah has dabbled in pornography, filming an adult film with porn star James Deen, as well as music and writing. She published a memoir My Teenage Dream Ended in 2012 and released a studio album that same year.

Best Known For:
Farrah Abraham is best known for her appearance on MTV’s first installment of Teen Mom.

Personal Life:
Farrah Abraham welcomed her daughter, Sophia Abraham, on Feb. 12, 2009. Sophia’s dad was Farrah’s high school boyfriend, Derek Underwood, who tragically died in a car accident when Farrah was 6 months pregnant. She was arrested in June 2018 after allegedly hitting an employee at a Beverly Hills hotel.
